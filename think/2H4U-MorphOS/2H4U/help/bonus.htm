Bonuses and Penalties



Bonuses



Increases the size of your "bat" or "racket"
Adds another ball to the game
Decreases the speed of the ball
Gives you extra bonus points
Allows you to move the Tetris pieces (if you got the corresponding penalty which disables you from doing so)


Penalties



Decreases the size of your "bat" or "racket"
Increases the speed of the ball
Disables you from moving the Tetris pieces.
