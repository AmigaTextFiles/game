Game Buttons



Below is a chart summarizing the buttons that you'll need to play 2H4U :

Upper part

Key Action
W Rotation of the bricks
A The bricks move left
D The bricks move right
S Accelerate the fall of the bricks
"Space" Drop the bricks down

Bottom part

Key Action
-> Move the racket to the right
<- Move the racket to the left
