Requirements
------------
This program has undergone testing on a standard A1200 and also an A1200
with an 030 accelerator and 16 meg FastRAM. It requires an AGA Amiga due
to the Coplist instructions used.

Installation
------------
Just Copy The Discworld drawer to the location of your choice. No assigns
are necessary.

Usage
-----
Just click on the main program icon to run it. The game is simple to operate
and quits back to workbench.
