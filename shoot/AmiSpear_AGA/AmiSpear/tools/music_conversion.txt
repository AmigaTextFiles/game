To generate Music and SFX packs will need the following conversion Steps on a Windows PC:

1) First run WolfExtractor (Wolfenstein Redux) to extract all the SFX and music
2) Convert the SFX files under the 'lsfx' directory to a lower quality (11025 Hz, 8 bit) using r8brain.exe
3) Convert the IMF music files to MIDI using dro2midi.exe
4) Finally you'll need to convert all of the MIDI files to MUS format to enable AmiWolf to play them (Wolfenstein Redux)
