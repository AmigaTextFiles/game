Requirements

Minimum Requirements

  • An Amiga with at least one megabyte of chip RAM
  • OS3.0+

Suggested System

  • RTG Graphics board with Picasso96 or CyberGraphX
  • 8Megs (or greater) of FAST RAM (ie a usable Amiga RTG setup)
  • OS3.1
  • Multisync monitor
  • 68030 processor or greater
  • A Web Browser such as IBrowse, AWeb, Voyager, etc. (to read the HTML dox ;-)
  • A three (3) button mouse
  • MUI 3.8 (for StrategoPrefs - a ToolType preferences program by Chris Page)

NOTE: You are also required to email us if you like this game!
