Welcome to the eVe preview version

eVe is an action/RPG game that is presented in a classic game style.
This preview edition is a work in progress snapshot of the current
build. It's meant to give the player a taste of some basic game elements.
This preview allows you to walk around the initial of the game
while interacting with a few characters and enemies. Expect the
storyline and gameplay elements to expand in future updates.


- Documentation -
-----------------------------------------------------------------

- Requirements

eVe requires a PPC computer running MorphOS v1.4x

- Keyboard and Gamepad controls -

eVe can be controlled with a keyboard or gamepad.
Gamepads are supported either through Poseidon
or lowlevel.library depending on connection type.

Keyboard Controls:

arrow keys: menu navigation and character movement
x : Cancel / Menu
z : Confirm
spacebar : Use item

GamePad Controls:
(The gamepad can be remapped by using the in-game config menu)
D-Pad : Move your character and move menu cursors
Button A : Cancel / Menu
Button B : Confirm
Button C : Use item
Button D : Unused / Reserved

- How To Play -

eVe is an action roleplaying game where you must search for clues,
interact with other characters and defeat various foes to unravel
the mysteries presented in the game.

You can move you main character "Cleall Noble" using the d-pad
to navigate the terrain. To speak to other characters simply
bump into them and proceed through the conversations by pressing
any button on the gamepad. You can also enter buildings by walking
through an opened door. Inside buildings you are presented with
various scenes including shops where you can purchase important
items.

- Combat -

Fighting is accomplished by running up and "bumping" into your
enemies. By hitting them off-center you will gain an advantage
and have a better chance of damaging the enemy. You can also
improve your chances by obtaining weapons and increasing
character levels. Each time you defeat an enemy you will gain
experience points that will automatically raise your general
abilities on each level up achieved. Many enemies also give
a certain amount of gold coins when defeated. Gold coins can
be used to purchase various items within shops.

- Healing -
When in a reasonably safe area like an outdoor scene you can
simply sit still for a moment to let your Hit Points refill
automatically. In the full game you will not be able to heal
inside of dangerous locals. If you are in an area where healing
is not available it's advisable to carry a potion or other
method of healing.

- Items and Equipment -
You must purchase or find various items during the course of
the game. Weapons, shields armor and bracelets can be equipped
by highlighting them in the equip menu.

Items are readied by putting the cursor over the item and then
pressing the confirm button. You can attempt use a readied item
by pressing the spacebar or item button. Some items are used
automatically depending on the situation and don't need to be
activated manually. (keys and other unique items)


- Description of Game Menus -

You can open the in-game menu by pressing the Cancel/Menu button.
The menu is closed by pressing Cancel again.

Equip  - Set which equipment you want Cleall to wear
Item   - Ready an item for use or just browse
Status - Display your current statistics
Load   - Load a previously saved game
Save   - Save your game progress in place
Config - Configure/remap the default gamepad buttons
Quit   - Quit back to the main menu
