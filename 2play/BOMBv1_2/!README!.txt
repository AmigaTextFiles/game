Battle of The Master Bombers is a winner of the Amiga Format AMOS Games
Competition (AF75). It has since been updated to version 1.21, and is the
world's first 'Dynablaster clone' to include serial link capability, which
allows widescreen play !

Unregistered BOMB v1.2 features:

        o  Choice of eight different players
        o  1, 2, 3, or 4-Player game
        o  Support for parallel port 4-Player joystick adaptors
        o  Many exciting power-ups
        o  Control over gameplay options
        o  9 different playing areas
        o  'Stuart Campbell' Mode
        o  Atmospheric intro
        o  Kicking background music
        o  Detailed AmigaGuide®
        o  OS-friendly Commodore Installer®
        o  On-line support on IRC

If playing from HD, please use the installer provided, or unarchive this
LHA direct to floppy for playing off disk.

The default options with the confusing 'Stu Campbell' Mode has now been fixed.
Also tested and modified to work on A600s and similar. New hidden screen.

BOMB has been converted to Archimedes and IBM-PC. More information
available on E-Mail request.
All comments, bug reports, compliments, and A4000Ts accepted - feel free to
E-Mail us !
