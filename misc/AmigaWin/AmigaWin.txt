Requirements
------------
An AGA Amiga.

Installation
------------
Just copy the executable to the location of your choice.

Usage
-----
It's a fruit machine, nuff said. Oh yeah, to quit press escape or click on 
the tiny Q gadget in the top left hand corner.


Commodore * 3 = £ 0.50
Yin Yang  * 3 = £ 1.00
Check     * 3 = £ 1.00
Sphere    * 3 = £ 2.00
Bar       * 3 = £ 5.00
Skull     * 3 = £ 7.50
Gateway   * 3 = £10.00

Also, if you get a matching pair of symbols on the first two reels you win
20p.
