This is a period tripler reaction, with a glider gun to demonstrate it.
It accepts input gliders arriving at any multiple of 30 generations,
and emits one glider for every three input gliders.  Multiple copies can
be stacked together to form a glider gun with an arbitrarily large period.
David I. Bell, Jan 1996
