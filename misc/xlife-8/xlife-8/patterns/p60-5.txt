A period 60 glider gun using a mold to inhibit the output of a p30
glider gun.  The spark provided by the mold can be replaced by the
spark from other period oscillators, to form a range of glider gun
periods.  When this is done, another glider gun is needed to do a NOT
function of the output.
David I. Bell, February 1995
