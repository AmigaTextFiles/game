AdventureMiniDash is a Boulder Dash based game.

Multitasking, HD installable.

System requirements: Amiga with 512kB of memory is enough to play the game,
512kB Chip + 512kB (slow)Fast needed to hear the sound effects, 1MB Chip
required to see and hear the Intro. RGB monitor recommended, the game runs
in hires-lace screen. Joystick and mouse required, too. Keyboard would be
nice, not to mention power supply and a Disk/Hard drive to load the game.
External amplifier and decent loudspeakers makes sound effects to be EFFECTS.
