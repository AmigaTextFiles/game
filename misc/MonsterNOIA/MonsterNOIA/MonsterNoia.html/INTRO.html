INTRODUCTION

THE BACKGROUND

The initial idea was to look whether I could write a bigger project, for example a game, in my preferred programming-language, BlitzBasic (in the beginning it was Blitz->I<, which even less people might know now..:) ). I was a bit frustrated when I saw that I had only wrote little programms to test out the many features Blitz has, but when thinking of what useful or "kewl" software I had produced, nothing came into my mind...:(

THE IDEA IS BORN

So I said to myself, "let's try" and over the years (yes, I know, it was quite a long period of time;) ) when I had time to relax and get creative (mostly in the summer- or christmas-holidays), I took the project another step beyond, and *this* is what came out of it:-)

CONCLUSION

This is the *first* game I ever wrote, and after all the fun (but also *hard* work I had, I promise: it will not be the last..!
