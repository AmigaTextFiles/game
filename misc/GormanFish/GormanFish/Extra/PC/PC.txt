Gorman Fishtank **IBM PC Edition**
----------------------------------


Knocked up in a few minutes of boredom in Visual Basic, this is a very early
conversion of the Fishtank to the IBM PC and compatibles.

To run it, simply extract the ZIP to your hard drive and double-click its
icon.


Requirements
------------


 - Windows 95/98
 - An IBM PC or compatible
