THE DEVILS ABODE

This is a virtual reality 3D game for the A12OO and should work
with other Amigas too.

You were dared to enter the graveyard by your friends.You notice
the crypt so you are curious to explore it.To enter you must find
the Spell of Revelation which will allow you to enter.This can
be found if you look around the graveyard.

Once inside it's up to you to explore!

Best of luck!
