               How to control Cowie
               --------------------
General
-------
After some credits comming up you can see the game-prologe, just for
knowing why you fight.
Then press Mousebuttom for choosing 1player (F1) or 2players (F2).
If you choose 2 players, you have to plug in 2 joysticks, one player
moves our hero, the other do the fights (with firebuttom), nice!

Totally you have 3 lives, but you can get xtra lives from the objects-
map.
You don't have to begin from beginning all the time, after a while you
will get some passwords.

Objects-map
-----------
When playing, you can collect some stuff for better skills, for doing
this, just take the milk. When you have collected enough milk so one
object is lit, press <space> and you have chosen something. Please 
notice that your xtra skills will disepear if the game is over, even Ёf 
you have passwords. 

The skills you can collect is:
 Pause - For pausing the game                     (1 time)
 Clock - For get some more time              (1 time)
 Bomb  - For kill all enemies on screen           (1 time)
 Wings - For jumping higher - 3 different levels! (->dead)
 Shoe  - For moving faster  - 3 different levels! (->dead)
 Leg   - For kicking stronger                (->dead)
 Cow   - For get an Xtralife

If you for example have a very nice Jumpskill, and take another, then you will get 
this instead.


Joystick-info (WITHOUT firebuttom)
----------------------------------
Left  - To go left
Right - To go right
Up    - To Jump
Down  - To Duck


Joystick-info (WITH firebuttom)
-------------------------------
Direction Left       and Fire - Normal kick, Left direction
Direction Right      and Fire - Normal kick, Right direction

Direction Up+Left    and Fire - High kick,   Left direction
Direction Up+Right   and Fire - High kick,   Right direction

Direction Down+Left  and Fire - Low kick,    Left direction
Direction Down+Right and Fire - Low kick,    Right direction
