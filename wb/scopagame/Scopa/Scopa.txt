Scopa Game  v1.1


INTRODUCTION

Scopa is a popular Italian card game.
In this digital version the player will play
against the artificial intelligence of the PC.
The game is won by the player or PC who first
reaches or exceeds the score of 11


USE
To launch it, just double click on its icon.
The game was written in C ++ and requires no installation.
The game, once launched, will open a window.
To start playing you will need to press the [START] button.
