INTRODUCTION
Catch It translated into Italian "Acchiapparello" like the analogous
game boyish.
The aim of the game is not to get caught while searching
to take the key to open the door that will take us to the room
next of the basement of the castle, that is to the next level.

There are 10 levels and through a specific Options menu item
it is possible to have infinite lives.

It is also possible to use the joystick.


USE
To launch it, just double click on its icon.
The game was written in C ++ and requires no installation.
The game, once launched, will open a window.
To start playing you will need to press the [START] button.
