.------------------------------.
| Before making custom puzzles |
`------------------------------'

If you are wanting to make your own puzzles then use a 153x153
area and 256 colours for the best results. You can also change
your puzzle settings by creating a config file for it. Remember
that all puzzles end with the suffix ".puz" and config files end
with ".cfg".

If your saving your puzzles as a non-ILBM format, remember that PNG,
GIF and now JPG is the recognised standard for WBPerplexity. Please
try not to use any formats such as PCX, BMP, TARGA and TIFF etc.

.-----------------.
| Config settings |
`-----------------'

TEXT=[Message] :Sets the message to display in the puzzle info requester.

TITLE=[Title] :Sets the puzzles title

BUTTON=[Text] :Sets the puzzle information "OK" button. Defaults to "Proceed"
