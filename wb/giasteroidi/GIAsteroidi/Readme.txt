INTRODUCTION

Game in which a butterfly has to collect diamonds and avoid
the asteroids. At each level the background changes showing 
churches and monuments of Cerreto Sannita (a small village in 
the Sannio in province of Benevento - Italy).


USE
To launch it, just double click on its icon.
The game was written in C ++ and requires no installation.
The game, once launched, will open a window.
