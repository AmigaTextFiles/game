INTRODUCTION
Tressette a Perdere is a popular Italian card game.
In this digital version the player will play against the 
artificial intelligence of the PC which will be divided 
into three opponents.
The game ends when one of the players reaches 51 points 
and is won by the player who scores the fewest points.

The Ace of Sticks is worth 11 points.

USE
To launch it, just double click on its icon.
The game was written in C ++ and requires no installation.
The game, once launched, will open a window.
To start playing you will need to press the [START] button.
