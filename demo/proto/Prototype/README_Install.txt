PROTOTYPE
---------
Programmed by Christopher Caleb
Graphics by Alan Caleb
Music by Simon Sinclair
Sound Sampling by Christopher Caleb, Alan Caleb and Simon Sinclair
Vocal Performance by Simon Sinclair


Specification
-------------
Prototype will run on any Amiga with 2Mbyte of RAM

Recommended Spec:
'020 Amiga (or higher) with at least 4Mbyte of RAM and a hard disc

IMPORTANT
This demo of Prototype can only be played from hard disc


Installation
------------
1. Uncompress the LHA file onto your hard disc. If you are reading this,
   then you must have already uncompressed the LHA file.
2. Go inside the PROTOTYPE directory and click on the HD_INSTALL icon.
   This will copy some relevent fonts and libraries into the relevent
   directories on your hard disc.
3. The game is now ready to run. Simply click on the PROTOTYPE icon.

If for any reason the installer fails, then you can copy the relevent files
from inside your PROTOTYPE directory over to the proper directories on your
hard disc using the shell.

Just copy over the contents of PROTOTYPE/libs PROTOTYPE/fonts PROTOTYPE/devs
etc to LIBS: FONTS: and DEVS: etc on your hard disc.
