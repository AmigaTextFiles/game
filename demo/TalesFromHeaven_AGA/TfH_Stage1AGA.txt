Demo Controls:

Joystick --- Move Zaac (the main character) and jump with fire button
SPACE ------ Left Bombs
ESC -------- Options Menu




Features:

- RTG Compatible;
- 2 button joystick support;
- Many soundtracks and stereo sound effects;
- 68040 and 68060 optimized routines;
- More than 2000 polygons on screen simultaneously;
- 3 Worlds with 4 levels each;
- Over 2 years of work and 8 people behind it.
