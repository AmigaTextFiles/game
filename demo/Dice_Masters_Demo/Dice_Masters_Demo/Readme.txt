Pixel Art Tower Defence !

You accidentally fall into a dungeon, this triggered
a silent alarm on a spaceship full of aliens. They 
are monitoring you and launching a full-scale attack 
to protect their secret dungeon earth base ! You 
can't escape, only way is forward... Good Luck !           

Have fun !!! 



Arcade mode endless
Story mode 10 levels
Arrow - move cursor               
Space - action                      
USB Joypad support, D-pad, A, Start, Back
Game done in assembler 


Requirement: 

Apollo Vampire V4 
AmigaOS3.x / ApolloOS
640x360x32
