To learn how to control your characters and their actions start the game
then press the ESC key. Choose Game Controls from Game Menu and read the
instructions carefully.



                               Walk through:
                               =============
Scene01:
--------
 1. Pickup empty bottle.
 2. Go up and pass through hole to Scene02.

Scene02:
--------
 3. Kill all enemies.
 4. Examine enemy corps (you'll get charged electrifier).
 5. Examine stone bridge.
 6. Use (Return key) charged electrifier on stone bridge.
 7. Cross the river and kill all enemies.
 8. Throw empty bottle in the river (near sign).
 9. Go back to Scene01 or switch to another solider (F1-F4).
10. Pickup full bottle (near the river).
11. Return to Scene02 and proceed to Scene03 on the left.

Scene03:
--------
12. Run between rocks to escape the gun shots.
13. Use charged electrifier on the door.
14. Enter through to the Scene04.

Scene04:
--------
15. Kill all enemies.
16. Examine enemy corps (you'll get silver CD).
17. Use full bottle on the machine on the left.
18. Pickup steel bar from the floor.
19. Use silver CD on the terminal.
20. Choose System Protection then Shield System then Shields Off.
21. Exit from the terminal.
22. Use steel bar on the terminal.
23. Pickup empty hologram engine.
24. Combine ID card with empty hologram engine (that will turn on the hologram engine).
    (To combine objects hold down Alt key and press fire on first object then
     select second object and press fire on it, now leave the Alt key. You
     can use Space key instead on holding down Alt key if you prefer.)
25. Return to Scene03.
26. Walk through the canyon on the left and enter Scene05.

Scene05:
--------
27. Quickly put (Return key) tuned hologram engine on the ground and walk aside.
28. Shoot down the killer machine.
29. Shoot the canyon walls (left and right).
30. Collect 5 stones from the ground.
31. Pickup tuned hologram engine.
32. Return to Scene02.

Scene02:
--------
33. Throw again empty bottle to the river.
34. Throw 5 stones to the river too.
35. Now go back to the Scene01 and pickup full bottle.
36. Go again to the Scene05.

Scene05:
--------
37. Walk over crashed killer machine.
38. Kill all enemies.
39. Go right-up to the Scene06.

Scene06:
--------
40. Kill all enemies.
41. Examine enemy corps (you'll get recharge unit).
42. Use charged electrifier on captured human.
43. Use full bottle on captured human.
44. Talk to human.
45. Pickup security card from dead human body.
46. Combine recharge unit with electrifier.
47. Walk down between bushes.

Scene02:
--------
48. Now you are again on Scene02 but on the other side of the river.
49. Go right and use charged electrifier on the reactor ball.
50. Pickup reactor core.
51. Go back between woods to Scene06.

Scene06:
--------
52. Use security card on the door on the left building.
53. Enter the building.

Scene07:
--------
54. Go up and enter to the lift.
55. Proceed up to activate the lift.

Scene08:
--------
56. Use steel bar on the lifts right edge to block the door.
57. Exit from the lift and kill all enemies.
58. Use silver CD to access the computer terminal in the center of the room.
59. Choose Security Measures then Enable Outdoor Passage.
60. Exit from the terminal.
61. Use the lift on the right side of the room.


Scene09:
--------
62. Proceed down and exit from this room.

Scene06:
--------
63. Proceed up between two buildings.
64. End of DEMO.
