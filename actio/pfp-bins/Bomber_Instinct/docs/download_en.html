    Warning : the game is'nt finished yet, and some character animations, sound effects, musics, are missing... It's far more than playable however and stable (at least on my computer).
    To compile from the source, you will need the SDL library and SDL_mixer for the sound. Once the two installed, uncompress the archive ("tar -xzf BomberInstinct-xxx.tar.gz") and do a "./configure", "make" followed by "make install" (as root) in the BomberInstinct directory to install the game. To play the game type "bi"; you can also edit the levels and sprites with "nived" and "spred".
