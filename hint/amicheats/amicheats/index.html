A Bit About Me
Hi ! I am a married 31 year old male with one son that lives near Windsor, Ontario, Canada. I started using computers at about the age of 5 with a Commodore Vic-20. It was just a game machine to me, but then i moved up to the Commodore-64 which i programmed to death. In the mid-80's i got my first Amiga and it was love at first mouse click. At first the Amiga was a graphics and sound toy, now i also do some programming. Through extensive reading, friends, use and work i've also become adept with DOS and Windoze computer platforms. Of coarse, computers aren't my only interest. I also enjoy working out, sci-fi, volley ball, the outdoors, reading, and learning everything i can about everything. I do a great deal of reading about computers and any sort of scientific topic, whether it be, astronomy, partical physics, relativity, quantum mechanics, geology, archeology, meteorology, phsycology, etc... etc... I'd also like to note that the internet, and specifically the World Wide Web, has greatly increased my ability to find new and wonderful topics to learn about.
