Outrun

Produced by: Sega
Distributed by: Mindscape Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Take the ride of your life in the home-computer version of Sega's arcade megahit. This baby's sleek, smooth... the fastest thing on four wheels. Floor the gas pedal and let her ride past the beaches of southern France, California's Death Valley, cities, mountains, and vineyard.

CHEATS

During play, type "RED BARCHETTA" now press any of the following:

'S'-----Skip levels
'T'--Add 10 seconds
'B'---Extended play

The following may be for another version:

Type "STARION" while playing the game. Now use the following keys:

'B'----------------Extended play
'S'------------------Next screen
'T'-------------Extra 10 seconds
'X'-------------------------Quit
'D'--Save screen in Degas format
'Q'-----------------Program info
