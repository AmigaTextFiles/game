DESCRIPTION

Beamed down to the planet Ixion from the Legion Ship, Cisskel, aspiring Captain Ku-Kabul has to face the dangers and tests specifically placed on this planet in order to prove himself worthy of leadership. Failing any of the tests will end his career as a Legion-Command Officer... permanently! To give him a fighting chance, Ku-Kabul is fitted with twin laser cannons and refuelable jet boosters. Using brains and brawn, he must find, collect, and use objects to solve the many perplexing puzzles and defeat the hordes of deviously powerful enemies that infest this deadly arena. Powerful arcade special effects including full-screen three-layer parallax scrolling running arcade speed, larger than screen aliens, and a haunting musical score combine with furious gameplay to bring you an incomparable arcade-like gaming experience.

CHEATS

Stand by a computer terminal and pull down and press fire. Now point the cursor in all four corners of the options screen. The cheat mode will now be active. This enables these keys:

'F'-----------------------Extra fuel
'H'----------Refills your life meter
'A'-----------------Extra ammunition
'RETURN'--Take off and land anywhere

LEVEL CODES

The code to gain access to level three is: "2314". Use the joystick to enter the code at the end of level two.
