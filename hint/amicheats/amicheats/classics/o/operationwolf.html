Operation Wolf

Produced by: Taito Software, Inc.
Distributed by: Taito Software, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

When terrorists must be stopped, Operation Wolf gets started. This commando-style attack adventure explodes with action from start to finish. In the steamy jungles of South America, heavily armed extremists are holding innocent civilians hostage. The only way to rescue the hostages is to get in, strike hard, and get out, FAST!

CHEATS

At the end of a level, between the time when you kill the last bastard and the time appears, rolling the mouse down causes damage to decrease. For the most accurate shot possible, pause the game with 'F1', position your crosshairs over the selected enemy, unpause the game, click the button and blast him to hell.
