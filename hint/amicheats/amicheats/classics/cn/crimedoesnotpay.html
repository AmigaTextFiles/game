Crime Does Not Pay

Produced by: Titus Software Corporation
Distributed by: Titus Software Corporation

DESCRIPTION

Or does it? This is the official mobster game endorsed by the Mafia. You don't own the town yet? That won't last long. Maybe becoming mayor of the city would solve your problem. But what about using mobsters to bribe, blackmail, and fight the big guys. Don't listen to justice and order, have no scruples, and lead your gangs to gain control over money and power. Who are you? The godfather, the femme fatale, or the killer? You will have a realistic map of the city where you can visit 200 locations and meet 10 important people.

CHEATS

Pause the game and type "HALTBAKK IS THE KING" for unlimited lives.
