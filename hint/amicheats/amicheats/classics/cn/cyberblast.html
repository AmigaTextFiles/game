Cyberblast

Produced by: Innerprise Software, Inc.
Distributed by: Innerprise Software, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Your goal is to make your way through 64 levles of Fastrax Labs until you find the missing control chip. Locating the control chip closes the portal that allows the deadly aliens to enter our universe. To complete a level, you must find and grab the crystals and then locate and enter the exit within a specific time limit. You pick up energy, points, laser pistols, bombs, and grenades. Each character has unique speed, shields, strenght, zappower, and shotspeed. Of course our journey is hampered by walls, enemies, and many other threats to your life. Two-player simultaneous mode.

CHEATS

On the title screen, type "LAURIUS" for extra energy. Press 'N' to advance levels. Press 'L' for the last level. This cheat also give you infinite extras.
