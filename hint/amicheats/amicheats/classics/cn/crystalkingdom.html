CHEATS

Enter these codes into the game genie:

"F7J7G8FD8"--Infinite lives
"FC9036B47"----Faster Dizzy
"G5J73Q8HK"---------Level 2
"H7T554Y9L"---------Level 3
"83J5G62KF"---------Level 4

It is possible to activate both the infinite lives and the speed. After you enter one of the codes the game will start. Just press 'ESC' and go back to the game genie and enter the other code. You can also do this and enter a higher level code.

PART 1: THE YOLKFOLK VILLAGE

Climb up to Denzil's Hut and jump left from the edge, you land on a cloud, continue jumping left otherwise you fall thru the cloud. On the other side grab the Screwdriver and a Peanut, then go back to Denzil's Hut. Talk to denzil and he takes the screwdriver.

Take the spanner and go all the way down and right, give peanut to CJ the elefant, and continue right, use the spanner on the generator. Go to the lift and jump right to Daisy's, go in thru the window and take the whip.

Now go up to the village and enter the hut in the right corner (Dora's Kitchen) talk to dora and take the cake, then go back to CJ and give him the cake, take the umbrella and walk left to the waterfall.

Enter the cave and grab the diamant, the go right to the well, and use the diamant, then the genie will appear and you make a wish.

Go back up to denzil's hut and take the glasses, go outside and jump up to the next platform twice, you should be on the top middle platform in the village, jump strait up and you see a platform to your right jump up on it, then place dizzy on the left edge and press fire and you will swing over to the other platform, go left and jump the the hut and enter.

Talk to dylan and take the news paper, then go back to the village and enter the hut in the middle of the screen. Talk to Grand Dizzy and he tell you the news in the paper.

Walk right and enter Fat Freddies Shop, talk to freddie and he will give you a can of tunafish, leave the shop and continue right.

At the pier throw the tunafish in the water, talk to dolphin, then jump on it's back and it will take you to the ship, jump up on the anker and climb up, talk to captain beardy.

Climb up the pole til you reach the aft sail, take tape, walk right to the edge and jump right (keep joystick to the right until you land) then climb the pole until you reach the grow's nest, take the blu-tak and climb down.

Place the tape next to the door and walk right to the bow and take the saw, go left to fore sail bottom and climb the pole, then take the steering wheel and go down and left to the helm, use the blu-tak on the wheel holder? and you fixed the steering wheel, go right to mast bottom take the tape and enter the door, You need to find a plank of wood, leave all other items you find for now, when you got the wood find the bilges then use the plank of wood on the hole. go up the ladder to your right then go left and take the needle and thread, then go back to the place where you found the plank of wood.

Take the sheet from the captain's bed and head for the exit, take the scissors on the way to.

from the mast bottom go left to the captain and climb the pole, walk to the right edge and jump out (joystick right all the way down) and climb the pole, You have to cut all the bindings so the sail is down. You can see a hole in the sail, go to the hole and press fire and you fix the hole if you have the following things (needle + thread, sheet & scissors) go to the captain and talk to him.

Climb down the chain and jump on the dolphin's back again and it brings you back to the pier, go to Fat Freddies shop and enter, go up the stairs and exit thru the window, jump right,right,right,left,right take superglue,jump left,left,left,left,use scissors on hammock, talk to dozy and take the flag, now go back to the pier and let the dolphin take you back to the ship.

Climb the pole next to the captain and jump from the edge as you done twice before, and then climb to the top and place the flag in the nest. Go back down to the captain, take badge and climb down the chain to the dolphin.

Walk to Fat Freddies and talk to him, drop the scissors and take the map & the telescope, run back to the ship, and give the map to the captain, then climb up to the grow's nest, then use the telescope.
