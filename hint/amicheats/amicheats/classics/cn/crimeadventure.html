Crime Adventure

SOLVE

NOTES

The following items can be done for fun, but don't need to be done to finish the game.

  • Pay gypsy woman a penny so that she tells you what the author has already told you. For example, your mission.
  • Get her painting and she'll throw you out.
  • Hit the arcade machines twice and you'll get thrown out.
  • Go east or west on the eastwest roads and you'll have an accident.

The following items are not used in the game: hat, painting, license plate, gypsy mirror.

START

Examine fortune cookie in restaurant (tells you how to find Mrs. Fenwick). But Mr. Fenwick won't give you his putter.

Read Mrs. Fenwick's diary. Read computer in IBM (for stew recipe). Get all the stuff in Fenwick kitchen. Make stew. Feed Mr. Fenwick (to get putter).

Examine dresser (to get cash). Read Fenwick note and Dig ground with shovel (get coin). Insert coin in PAC-MAN. Buy pair of golf shoes from shoe store.

Get Hairpin and Drop golf ball in green before putting ball. You are now in an underground passage.

Pick lock with hairpin. Get chair and Drop it below the ceiling hole. Climb chair and go in dining room.
