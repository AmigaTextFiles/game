Cyberpunks

CHEATS

During play, hold down 'R', 'G', 'B', the LEFT MOUSE BUTTON, and the FIRE BUTTON. The screen should flash to let you know it worked. This gives you infinite energy and the use of the following keys:

'F1'-'F5'--Skip to corresponding level
'1'-'4'------Warp around current level
'A'--------Deploy stationary drone gun
'S'--------------------Activate shield
'D'-----------------------Deploy drone
'Z'----------Select red players weapon
'X'---------Select blue players weapon
'C'---------Select blue players weapon
'LEFT SHIFT'----------------Smart Bomb
