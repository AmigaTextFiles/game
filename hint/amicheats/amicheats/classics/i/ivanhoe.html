Ivanhoe

CHEATS

Pause the game and type "ZOBINETTE". The screen will flash to let you know the cheat is activated. Now unpause the game and press any of the following keys.

'M'---------------------------For extra lives
'N'------------------------Skip to next level
'DEL'-------------Kills all enemies on screen
'CTRL'--Kills the big boss in the bonus stage

If the above doesn't work try "JC IS THE BEST" in the same place.
