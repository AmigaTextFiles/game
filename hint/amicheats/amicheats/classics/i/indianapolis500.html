DESCRIPTION

Players compete against 32 other cars in races ranging from 10 laps to the full 200 lap race. You can also practice before the race and then enter the qualifying rounds to try for the Pole position. Eight different systems on the car can by adjusted and saved to disk for later use, including shock settings, wing angles, and tire selection. Accidents on the course create spectacular special effects and can be viewed from multiple angles using Instant Replay. Create and save up to three custom cars or join one of the three teams.
