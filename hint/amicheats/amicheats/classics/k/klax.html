Klax

CHEATS

To play the last screen (level 100) hold down 'CTRL' + '4' during the game.

Begin play then hold down either of the 'SHIFT' keys and 'SPACE' together. Now press any key from '1' to '4' for different helpful effects, including infinite credits and even a free ticket to level 100 by pressing '4'.

While playing the game, press all the keys on the left hand side of the keyboard and you will skip to the next level.
