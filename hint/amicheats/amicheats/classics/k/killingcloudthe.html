Killing Cloud

Produced by: GameTek
Distributed by: GameTek

DESCRIPTION

Players assume the role of a high-flying San Francisco police officer in the year 1997. The Killing Cloud, a 100-foot thick mass of pollution, covers the earth. One breath of its fumes is fatal. The player must transport survivors to safety above the cloud in a XB500 hoverbike. Crime is rampant as all the old street gangs unite to form the Black Angels. The ultimate mission is to gain information on the Black Angels, break the stronghold they have on the city, and uncover the sinister mystery of the cloud.

CHEATS
