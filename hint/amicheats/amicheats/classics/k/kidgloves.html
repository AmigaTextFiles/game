Kid Gloves

CHEATS

During play, press 'F1' to pause the game, then type "RHIANNON". Then press one of the following:

'F9'----------------Invincibility
'F8'--For keys, spells, and money
'F7'-----------------Select level
'F6'----------To appear in a shop

(If you want more stuff you'll have to retype "RHIANNON" and press the desired key)
