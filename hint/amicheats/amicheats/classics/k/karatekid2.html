Karate Kid II

Produced by: MichTron/Microdeal
Distributed by: MichTron/Microdeal

DESCRIPTION

1 or 2 players, joystick or keyboard option, sound and graphics. You must discover the secret of the drum or die! Catch flies with chopsticks and break ice with your bare hand in full-size, animated graphic screens.

CHEATS

Enter your name as "MYAGI" in the high scores table. During game press 'P' to skip to the next level in 1 player mode. You can skip all the way up to the final encounter. In a two player game, you can skip to the second last screen.
