TIPS

There is a section in Chapter 4 where you have to go through the right door. The best thing to do is to save the game before you go through a door, and then if it is the wrong door just load the saved game and try again.

If you want to try and make any sense of the plot and what happens, then have a pen and paper ready and make a note of every major thing you find out, as it will help you to understand what has happened at the end of the game.
