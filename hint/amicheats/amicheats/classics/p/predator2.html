Predator II

Produced by: GameTek
Distributed by: GameTek

DESCRIPTION

Take on the role of veteran cop Mike Harrigan. Shoot it out with machine gun-toting drug lords and the Predator, a brutal alien who finds sport in the killing of human prey. Collect powerful weapons to save your fellow officers, then use streetwise ingenuity and devise a way to destroy the creature's invisibility mechanism. Each level is inspired by the movie with plenty of action to please arcade enthusiasts. Digitized movie scenes recapture the Predator just as he appears on the big screen.

CHEATS

Press 'P' to pause the game and type "YOU'RE ONE UGLY MOTHER". You will now be invincible and can skip level by pressing '1' on the main keyboard.
