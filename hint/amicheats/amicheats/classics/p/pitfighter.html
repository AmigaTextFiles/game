Pitfighter

Produced by: GameTek
Distributed by: GameTek

DESCRIPTION

You can be one of three players: TY - the Kickboxer, KATO - the 3rd degree Karate Black Belt, or BUZZ - ex pro wrestler. In order to become the new Pitfighting World Champion, you must fight to the death one of eight brutes: The Executioner, Southside Jim, Angel, Mad Miles, CC Rider, Heavy Metal, Chainman Eddie, and The Ultimate Warrior. You need strength, skill, cunning, and the ability to smash your opponent with anything you can grab from the pit before he smashes you!

CHEATS

Start the game and type "LOBSTERS", use the numeric key pad '1', '2', and '3') to advance to the corresponding level. Then try these keys:

'L'---------For elimination round
'G'--------------For grudge match
'C'--For final championship round
