CHEATS

On any table while the table is scolling up and down type any of the following (WITH spaces, no Return required):

"FAIR PLAY"-Disables previously entered cheats
"EARTHQUAKE"-----------------Disables the tilt
"EXTRA BALLS"------Five balls instead of three
"VACUUM CLEANER"--------Clears the high scores
"THE SILENTS"---------Message from the silents
"HIGHLANDER"-----------Makes your ball heavier
"TECH STUFF"-------------------Technical stuff
"CREW"---------Message from the technical crew
"ULF"------------------------Message about ulf
"OLOF"----------------------Message about olof
"MARKUS"------------------Message about markus
"ANDREAS"----------------Message about andreas
"BARRY"--------------------Message about barry
"FREDRIK"----------------Message about fredrik
"DIGITAL ILLUSIONS"---------Ball can't be lost (You can only leave the game by pressing 'P' to pause or 'ECS' to quit the table.)
