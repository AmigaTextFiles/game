Prison

Produced by: Actionware
Distributed by: Actionware

DESCRIPTION

The year is 3033. Criminals are no longer sent to jail, they're sent to a penal colony on the abandoned planet, Altrax. You have been wrongfully convicted of a crime and sent there. Your only hope for life is to find a rumored space pod and escape. Game play and lighting affected by Game Day-Cycle; communicate and interact with other game characters.

TIPS

Standing next to an object to pick it up is`t good enough. You have to be right in front of it.

The red light to the right of and below pocket one will light up when you enter a screen where there is something hidden.

A very important point. Save the game regulary!

Search packing cases after killing the droids.

In the room containing the booby-trap, search the unit to the left of the one that expoldes. Here you will find a detonating device. pull the red wire to disarm it.

Search the unit to the right to find explosives.

Drop the exploves on the rubble blocking the door, remembering to get out of the way when it goes off.

Jump two mines and one hole to find the room containing the stooge.

Trade the jewel found under the bed for a vending token.

Wear a tie and go to the night club to find a plank.

Place the plank on the edge of the large hole and jump the gap.

Put the token in the vending machine. Choose inner city as the destination and take the ticket to the guard.

Find plug two and take it up the first lift to open the door. Get plug three to open the locked door on the first level.

Take an object and wonder around the park until you meet a stooge.

Go to the agreed place at the correct time while carrying another object and swap it for the combination. Enter the vault and collect tne credit.

Go south-west from the bank and search near the dustbin.

Go past the teleport to find something useful.

Take it to the secretary and insert it. I know it sounds rude, but that`s what you do!

Go up in the lifts and search there. Use credit to teleport to next screen.

Find the vibe-o-mat and the fuel. Arm it and drop it near the row of mines now take the right hand lift in the building and search threr.

Find the room with a grille placed in the left hand wall. Fill the torch and burn away the gille.
