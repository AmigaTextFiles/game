Produced by: Broderbund Software Inc.
Distributed by: Broderbund Software Inc.

DESCRIPTION

You are a young adventurer from a far-off land, and the Sultan's beautiful daughter has fallen in love with you. But the Grand Vizier Jaffer has other plans. In the Sultan's absence, he has thrown you in prison, and given her an hour to choose: Marry him or die. You'll need acrobatic agility, superb swordsmanship, and brilliant puzzle-solving ability to save her. Twelve levels with more than 250 increasingly difficult screens.

CHEATS

Press these key combinations to do the following:

'SHIFT', 'K'-------------To kill guard
'SHIFT', '+'---------------To add time
'SHIFT', 'W'--To levitate when falling
'SHIFT', 'T'---------For full strength
'CAPS', 'L'-------------To skip levels
