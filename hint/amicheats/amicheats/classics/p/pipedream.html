Pipe Dream

Produced by: Lucasfilm Games
Distributed by: Lucasfilm Games

DESCRIPTION

Pit your pipefitting prowess against a surging sea of sewage. Your objective is to build the longest possible continuous pipeline, racing against a stream of chemical fluid called Flooz. Piece together the pipeline from various shapes of pipe, which appear in a random order. There are thirty-six increasingly challenging levels. Play solo, against a friend or team up in a joint battle against the computer. But if you slow down for a second, it's Sludge City!
