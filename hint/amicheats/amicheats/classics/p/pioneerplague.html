Pioneer Plague

Produced by: Bill Williams
Distributed by: Antic Publishing

DESCRIPTION

The first Amiga game in HAM mode is a new and exciting space adventure by Bill Williams. Pioneer Probe Mark IV, the self-replicating, terraforming, robotic spaceship, has gone out of control! Its original mission was to find suitable planets for the human race, but now you must stop the probe before it destroys all the planets including the Earth. Four monitors, status instruments, drones and photon missiles guide your way. HAM mode, dazzling graphics, 4096 colors multi-level design and more.

CHEATS

When you're on a planet, shoot as many rockets as you can. Now when the rockets are ready to go off, just press 'Q' and you'll be back on the ship. Then go to the planet. The rockets will still be there but the rockets will have reset. If you do this on all the planets, you can't lose. This cheat also works if you are about to die. Just press 'Q' before you explode. When you go back down you'll be as good as new.
