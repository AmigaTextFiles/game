DESCRIPTION

Populous II includes a cast of Greek deities. You approach the great god Zeus with aspirations of immortality. Your request is accepted only if you first prove your abilities by defeating 32 of the most powerful deities on the ancient Greek battlefields. With over 30 new animated powers, you will be twice the deity you were in the original Populous. Forces of nature can now be unleashed simultaneously. You can establish your deity's strengths, personality, and appearance. Different personality traits will alter the way the other deities react to your character. You're also responsible for the happiness of your populous if you want them to stay loyal. Planting trees, building roads, and making fertile land will all lead to increased manna. And face it, that's all you really want - the power to destroy!

CHEATS

Type "ADKITAKDVGZLRGWZ" at password prompt. This should give you maximum everything. It may be "ADKIUCMCZNDIFINL", "ADKIUCKBZNZEFIWX", or "ADKITDMEVQDPXWTN"

To activate lightning, hold down the Left Mouse Button, Press '1' on the keyboard, and release the Mouse Button. Now the lightning remains and your manna won't decrease.

Try typing in "MUSIC" as a special code for different music and click on the writing that goes round the game area for special effects.
