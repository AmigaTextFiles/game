PGA Tour Golf

Produced by: Electronic Arts
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

Enter the world of big-time golf. See TV-style graphics with aerial fly-bys every hole, multiple shots of the ball in flight, announcers, and pro tips. Tee off against 60 top PGA TOUR pros. Compete in real tournaments like the Kemper Open and THE PLAYERS Championship. Play the TPC's at Sawgrass, Avenel, and more. A 3-D putting grid, five types of swings, 12 ball lies, it even tracks your game with pro stats.

CHEATS

Before you tee off on the first hole click the button for the tournament statistics. Look through all of these and then play the game as usual and you'll find that your opponents have dropped quite a few shots. Do this on each hole, and by the end of the game the computer players will be quite a way behind you.
