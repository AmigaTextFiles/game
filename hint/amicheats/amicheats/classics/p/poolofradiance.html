Pool Of Radiance

Produced by: Strategic Simulations, Inc.
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

All monsters, items, and spells are from the famous AD&D Monster Manuals, Dungeon Masters Guide, and Players Handbook. Roll up your characters from four Classes, sic Races and nine Alignments in classic AD&D fashion. Personalize your heroes' combat figures with individual weapons, armour, and colors.

CHEATS

Once you find any item you can make as many copies of it as you want.

  1. Create a dummy character
  2. Load a saved game and transfer all the good items to the dummy
  3. Remove the dummy character from the party
  4. Load the dummy character back in
  5. Transfer the items to other members
  6. Drop the dummy character from the party
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 as many times as needed

Press 'ALT' and 'X' or 'ALT' and 'A' when the enemy is about to make his move, then a message will appear and you will win the fight.
