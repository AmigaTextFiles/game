PowerMonger

Produced by: Electronic Arts
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

PowerMonger takes you to a living world where each individual has a home, an occupation, and a level of intelligence - all must be accounted for in your strategy. PowerMonger casts you as the leader of a displaced tribe newly arrived in an unchartered territory. There are 200 territories to conquer before the world is yours. To unite the land under your rule, you must win the allegiance of the people by force, negotiation, or outright bribery.

CHEATS

Instruct your captain to invent something, pause the game, put the game speed up to full, and wait a couple of minutes. Now unpause the game, the captain will have kept inventing during the pause!

When you wish to invent in double quick time, click invent and when your men leave, click on it again. This often makes a catapult almost immediately, but this cheat only works once on each island.

FUN

In winter, take a lone captain to the north of the map. Leave him there, but keep an eye on him, because he'll start shearing a sheep. Then, he eats it.
