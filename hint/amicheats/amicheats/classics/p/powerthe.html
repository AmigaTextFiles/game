CHEATS

Hold down any of the following key combinations during play.

'CTRL' and 'B'---------Background scroll stops
'L SHIFT', 'R SHIFT' and 'T'--------Time stops
'L ALT', 'R ALT' and 'H'--All hearts collected
'L AMIGA', 'R AMIGA' and 'S'-------Skip levels
(only press the keys that are in single quotes)
