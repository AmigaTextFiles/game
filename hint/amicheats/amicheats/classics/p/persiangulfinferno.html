Persian Gulf Inferno

Produced by: InnerPrise Software, Inc.
Distributed by: InnerPrise Software, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Arcade action and role playing. Superb graphics and digitized sound. Excellent story - you must save hostages and disconnect a nuclear bomb on an oil rig.

TIPS

When you come across terrorists always remember to shoot the terrorists in front of you and keep running in the same direction. Do not worry about the terrorists behind you.

Every picture in this game is an IFF ILBM. You can change them using a painting program.

GAME CONTROLS

Joystick ---------------- movement and shooting
Function keys - weapon change (if you got them)
Space --------- blowing the doors (when locked)

SOLVE

Go to the stairs. Go up, go left to the lift. Go up (by lift) to 5th floor. Go right to door, open door, go right, open door, back to lift, and go up to 7th floor. Go right, open door, back to lift, go down to 4th floor. Go left to the end of corridor, and upstairs to the top floor. Go left, open door, back to stairs, down- stairs to the lowest floor. Go right, open door, go left to the end of corridor. Open door, back to stairs upstairs to higher floor. Go left to stairs, upstairs to the higher floor. Go left, open door, back to stairs, upstairs to 7th floor. Go left to the stairs, downstairs to 4th floor go left. Open door, go right to stairs, upstairs to 9th floor, go right to the end of corridor, and open door. Back to stairs, upstairs to 10th floor. Go left, open door, back to stairs. Downstairs to 6th floor, go left to the second lift, and go up to 12th floor. Go left, open door, back to lift, go up to 16th floor. Go right to lift, go up to 18th floor, go left to the end of corridor. Downstairs to 17th floor, go right, open door, and cut the green wire.
