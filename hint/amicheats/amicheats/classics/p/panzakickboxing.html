Panza Kick Boxing

TIPS

The best moves to use are upper cut, round house, low leg kick. The low leg kick is useful for keeping your opponent out of range and on target for a round house followed by an upper cut. The double backfist is also very powerful and often provides a knockout punch.

If you are losing a fight, and don't have much chance of recovery, then press the 'ESC' key so that your stats remain intact.
