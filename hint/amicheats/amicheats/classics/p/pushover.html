DESCRIPTION

G.I. Ant, a super-strong, super-smart soldier ant, is extremely clever and highly trained in tactical maneuvers. His latest mission is to gain access to the crazy world of Captain Rat's Treasure Caves. Straightforward? Not! This cave is a tunnel entrance to Domino Domain, a miniworld comprising nine different continents. G.I. must negotiate many screens of mind-teasing problems. Each level has a door to the next; to open it, the Trigger' domino block must be felled by causing a chain-reaction with all of the other domino blocks at that level. Easy? Not!
