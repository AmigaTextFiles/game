Switchblade II

CHEATS

On the title screen, type "LEVELn" and press fire to start on the corresponding level. Where 'n' is the level number like "LEVEL2" to go to level two.

Enter 'POOKY' into the high score table. Now while you're playing press one of the numerical keys (1 to 5) to warp straight to that particular level.

SUB GAME

On the title screen type "CHROME" to enter the sub game.

CHEATS

On the CHROME title screen, type "HELO TO GORDON AND DAVEE". Start the game and you will be invincible.

NOTE: The cheat for Chrome only works with the Switchblade 2 sub game. It doesn't work in the stand alone version!
