Starglider

Produced by: RainBird Software
Distributed by: RainBird Software

DESCRIPTION

Realistic flight simulation enables you to fly with incredible quickness, response time and unbeatable ease. Strategic missions with fast and furious action. Starglider brings together the reality of a flight simulator and an action-packed shoot-'em-up arcade game. High speed animation and spectacular vector graphics are so outstanding, you'll experience a true feeling of flight. Plus, the fast-paced mission (should you accept it) leads you into battle against a galaxy of bloodthirsty aliens.

CHEATS

Slow down the ship completely and press 'F1' for fixed sights. Press the 'BACKSPACE' to pause the game. Now type "JS ARG S" and hit return (twice). This will give infinite life, fuel, and shields. It may be JS(Space)ARG(Backspace)S(Backspace)(Return). Now use any of the following keys:

'P'--------Restock missiles
'E'--------Reboots the disk
'N'---Gives you 5000 points
'M'----Stops enemy aircraft
'Z'----Secret object viewer

To enter the secret object viewer, press 'z'. Then press 'p' to pause. Select '[', and ']' and manipulate objects. You can use all keys but 'q' and 'ESC'. Use the mouse to change point of view, or magnification with right mouse button.
