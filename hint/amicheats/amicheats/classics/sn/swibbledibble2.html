Swibble Dibble 2

CHEATS

When you get to the purple dragon, type "DIE" and he will drop a purple key. Take the key to Marvo and he will give you a cheat mode in return.

FUN

Typing "BLOOD" makes Mortal Kombat-style blood fly out of your enemies.

Typing "SUN" makes the sun wink at you.

Typing "WHAT A CROC" makes Dibble shout, when you do something dumb.
