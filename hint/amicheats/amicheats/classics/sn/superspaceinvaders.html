Super Space Invaders

Produced by: GameTek
Distributed by: GameTek

DESCRIPTION

The arcade smash of the 1970's is back with a vengeance. Combines a host of features from the arcade classic like Phoenix, Gorf, and Galaxians. Optimized graphics and sound as well as loads of new and exciting waves. Aliens swoop at you from all angles and in dozens of different attack formations. Advanced players can find secret hidden levels, and there are wacky bovine bonus levels. Instantly playable and totally addictive.

CHEATS

Type in "KRIS" on the option screen and you will then be able to skip through the levels with the 'F1' key.

When you collect the pod that produces four bases, placed at equal intervals along the bottom of the screen, don't just leave them there. If the invaders are quite low, shoot the bases up the screen and into the alien swarms. This way you are guaranteed of killing aliens as they die upon contact with bases, rather than allowing the space fiends to destroy them before they come down.
