Space Harrier

Produced by: Sega
Distributed by: Mindscape Inc.

DESCRIPTION

The home-computer version of this classic Sega fantastic space battle arcade hit. Zoom through a 3-D landscape as you fight off a myriad of bizarre creatures, including slithering serpents, fearless robots, and eerie stone masks throwing fireballs.

CHEATS

Enter your name as "RAF" into the second from the top place in the high score table for unlimited lives.
