S.W.I.V.

CHEATS

Pause the action and type "NCC-1701" then press the 'Return' key. The screen should flash blue indicating the cheat mode is active. This gives you infinite lives and you can select the various weapons by pressing 'F1' to 'F6'.

TIPS

The Goose helicopter from Silkworm is in S.W.I.V. as well, and just like before it is your only supply of weapons. All you have to do is to simply destroy it to get them - not as easy as it sounds. It's not too hard to see off, although you should be careful because it swoops down to the bottom of the screen every now and then and if you're in the way you'll be dead meat. However, by far the best way to destroy it is to collect a shield (if there's one handy) and just dive into the centre of it with all guns firing. The Goose will succumb very quickly this way and you don't take any risks.

Now, what are the best weapons to have? Well, don't bother with the compact bullets as they're not nearly as good as the spread fire. The best thing to do is to pick up a couple of spread fire and then grab hold of speed fire as well. The most you can get is five spread fire, so once you've got them concentrate on the speed fire.

The big Guardian you come across takes quite a bit to destroy. But with the right tactics you should be alright. When you approach the monster get on the left hand side and fire like mad. This should take out the small vehicle that comes out of the door on the left and also inflict a bit of damage on the Guardian (provided you took the advice on the weapon choice). Once the laser has stopped firing (it shoots three times before stopping) move over to the right and take out the small tank that came out of the other door. After the laser has fired again go back to the left and continue the process until the Guardian is destroyed. Remember, if you die you will be invulnerable for a few seconds so go on top of the Guardian and shoot like mad to inflict loads of damage. Just make sure you're not still there when you become vulnerable again.

The next Guardian you come across is quite a way into the game, but it makes up for the lack of them over the last loads of screens. Here you will be confronted by three huge lasers and two smaller gun turrets that will bombard you with a load of bullets every now and then. The first thing you should concentrate your fire on are the small gun turrets as they can locate where you are and make life very hard. Once they've been disposed of you can take your time over the lasers as they don't cause much of a problem.

Shields are very important in the game and should not be wasted. On no account use them as a smart bomb because this is a waste. The smart bomb will only kill the enemies on the screen and then you'll be under attack again. Using it as a shield will give you protection for quite a while, and is especially useful for tackling the Goose ship as mentioned before.
