Super Off Road

Produced by: Virgin Games, Inc.
Distributed by: Virgin Games, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Strap on your helmet, grit your teeth, and prepare to master mud heaven with Ivan 'Ironman' Stewart's Super Off Road. Shake, rattle, and roll your way through mud holes, jumps, matchoffs, and spin-outs in the closest thing to genuine short-course racing you'll ever experience. Race up to three players at the same time. Go wheel-to-wheel on eight different staduim tracks and a total of 16 configurations. Trade winnings for accessories at the speed shop to improve your chances at victory. Enough rough and tumble racing excitement to turn you into a leam, mean, off-road machine.

CHEATS

Simply hold down the fire button while the game is loading only releasing it when you come to the name/country selection screen. You will then find when you loose a race that you have infinite credits and money.

TIPS

Buying tires before level two will help you in turning corners.

Winning races by short margins will slow down the other cars in the next level.
