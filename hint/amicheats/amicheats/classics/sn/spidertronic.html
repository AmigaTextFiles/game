CHEATS

On the high score table, enter your name as "CHEAT", hit 'RETURN' and press the fire button. the screen will go black, a prompt will appear saying {LAST GAME LEVEL, GAME.LS0}. Type "GAME.LS" and the number of the level you wish to start. Press 'RETURN' and the fire button to start on that level.
