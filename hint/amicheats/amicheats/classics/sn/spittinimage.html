TIPS

The game is easiest if fought in this order:

  1. REAGAN, POPE
  2. GORBY, POPE
  3. AYATOLLAH, POPE
  4. BOTHA, POPE
  5. MARGARET, POPE

Finally a couple of useful pointers: keep close to your opponent, so they can't hit you back. Always move away from your opponent when their helpers appear, and hit your opponent with your special move, when they are using theirs.
