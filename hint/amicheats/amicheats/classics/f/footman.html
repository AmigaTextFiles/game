Footman

Produced by: Incognito Software
Distributed by: Incognito Software

DESCRIPTION

Many levels and lifelike sound make FootMan a classic arcade game. Two different games, simultaneous 2-player mode, and full maze also included.

CHEATS

In the first maze of {NEW WASTE}, go to the upper left corner entrance to the tunnel to hide from the ghosts and collect points from the fruit that appear periodically.
