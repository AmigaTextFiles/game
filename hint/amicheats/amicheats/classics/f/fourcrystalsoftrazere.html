DESCRIPTION

The land of Trazere has always been one of enchantment. That enchantment has taken a terrible turn; the Trazerians have been transformed into horrible mutants, bent on wreaking havoc. As your band of four arrives in this normally tranquil countryside, the transmogrification is immediately evident. But how did it happen, and can it be reversed? Four crystals hold the key, but it's up to you to determine how to use them.
