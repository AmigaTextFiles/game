Final Mission

Produced by: DigiTek Software
Distributed by: DigiTek Software

DESCRIPTION

Avoid enemies, collect objects. Dig and climb your way through 350 different levels. Dangerous mines, traps, and energy fields will push you to the limit. Excellent graphics, sound, and programming.

CHEATS

While the game is loading hold down the LEFT MOUSE BUTTON and 'T' until the game has totally loaded. This will give you infinite lives. (STAR COLLECTION)
