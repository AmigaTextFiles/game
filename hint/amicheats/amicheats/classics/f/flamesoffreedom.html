DESCRIPTION

The climatic disaster that precipitated a new Ice Age has finally diminished. As the ice and snow begin to melt, the people search for a home safe from the rising seas. But there is no paradise to be found, only the Saharan Empire - a brutal, totalitarian regime determined to control and manipulate everything in its path; an empire so powerful it can only be defeated from within. Your mission: promote discontent and revolution among the empire's populace through high-risk undercover operatians.

TIPS

To gain control of all the 41 islands, it's only necessary to capture the following: Lobos, Ndola, Camargo, Makat, Dhafia, Ghazal, Djoum, and Satara.
