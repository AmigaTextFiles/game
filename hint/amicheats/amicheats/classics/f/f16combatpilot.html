F-16 Combat Pilot

Produced by: Electronic Arts
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

Players must master five basic mission to become a Squadron Commander. Once all five missions are completed, players may undertake Operation Conquest, the final scenario. By successfully blowing away the enemy in Operation Conquest, players are promoted to a more elite squadron. There are nine squadrons in all, and each promotion becomes progressively more difficult to attain. The game includes LANTRIN-guided Maverick missiles, Iron bombs and Durandals, Advanced Tactical Air Reconnaissance System, and air-to-Air missiles. More than 500 fixed and mobile targets are spread over thousands of square miles on every mission.

CHEATS

If you`re fed up with landing try the following: Jetison all fuel and weapons and climb to 65000ft. Stay there until the last of your fuel runs out. Dive towards the ground and at 10 or 11 thousand feet pull up hard. You will then be able to rise about 4000 ft and then start falling towards the ground. Just as you hit the ground the mission will end safely.
