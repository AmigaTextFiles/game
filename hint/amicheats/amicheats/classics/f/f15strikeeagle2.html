F-15 Strike Eagle II

Produced by: MicroProse Software, Inc.
Distributed by: MicroProse Software, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

The classic game of aerial combat roars back to life in the '90s, loaded with fast action and vivid graphics. Dogfighting is the name of the game. Although the sky swarms with bogies, your streamlined weapons console targets enemy planes with the touch of a button to let you concentrate on the action. Enemy pilots and missile crews act and react intelligently. Success depends on making the right moves - fast!

CHEATS

During play, press 'CTRL', 'ALT', and 'R' simutaneously to restock your ammunition.
