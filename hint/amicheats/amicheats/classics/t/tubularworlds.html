Tubular Worlds

CHEATS

On the title screen when the title appears or on the options screen type "TODAZ IS HER BIRTHDAZ" if your using an American amiga or type "TODAY IS HER BIRTHDAY" if the Y and Z keys are switched: the Y be next to the X key. Now use the following keys during play:

'1-7'--Select various weapons
'S'---Gives you an orb shield
'H'---------Gives you rockets
'I'--Adds one to your shields
'E'----Toggles infinite lives
'L'------------------Not sure
'N'--------Skip to next level
'R'-----Restart current level

When you go to press one of the keys let off on the fire button.
