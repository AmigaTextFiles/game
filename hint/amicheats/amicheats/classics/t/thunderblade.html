Thunder Blade

Produced by: Mindscape Inc.
Distributed by: Mindscape Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Control the world's most sophisticated attack helicopter in the home-computer adaptation of Sega's #1 blockbuster arcade hit. Fly over skyscrapers, mountains, deserts and the sea as you attempt to accomplish the near-impossible mission of defeating the enemy.

CHEATS

On the high score table, type "CRASH" for your name. Press the 'HELP' key to skip levels.

To skip levels type in "CRASH" when the game has completely finished loading, and the picture from the film {Blue Thunder} appears. On pressing the 'H' key, the screen should flash to signify that the cheat is active. To access the next level press 'HELP'.
