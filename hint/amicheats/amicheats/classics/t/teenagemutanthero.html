Teenage Mutant Hero Turtles

NOTE: This game is also known as "Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles".

CHEATS

When entering the protection routine, type "8859" the first time, type "1506" the second time and then enter the correct code. The 'HELP' key toggles invincibility on and off. If you lose a turtle press 'HELP' to restore each turtle to full strength, even the dead ones.
