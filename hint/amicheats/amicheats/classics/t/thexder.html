Thexder

Produced by: Sierra On-Line
Distributed by: Sierra On-Line

DESCRIPTION

Pilot your armored robot through caves, cargo holds, and spaceship interiors. Transform Thexder into a jet. Over 20 kinds of aliens, hi-res artwork, synchronized soundtrack.

CHEATS

Turn on 'Caps Lock' and start the game. In play, press 'Esc', 'Y' then 'Esc' again and all your energy will be regained.
