Tower Of Babel

CHEATS

When collecting Klondikes, turn the grabber to face them and program the Grabber to 'Fire', 'Fire', 'Fire', 'Fire', 'Fire', 'Fire', 'Fire' and 'Fire'. The game thinks that you have collected EIGHT Klondikes. That means if the goal of the tower is just to collect Klondikes, you only have to get to the first Klondike!
