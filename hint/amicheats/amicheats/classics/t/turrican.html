Turrican

Produced by: Innerprise Software, Inc.
Distributed by: Innerprise Software, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Travel over 1300 screens, meet 50 different aliens, three-way parallax scrolling!

CHEATS

On the high score table, enter "BLUESMOBIL" for you name. On the next play you will see 99 lives on the bottom of the screen, and have loads of mines, lasers, etc.

If you come to a bad guy that you don't want to deal with, leave the screen and then come back. The bad guy should be gone. Unfortunately this also happens to extra men and weapons.
