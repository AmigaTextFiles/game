Rotor

Produced by: Microdaft
Distributed by: Microdaft

DESCRIPTION

A strategy-oriented arcade game, where you are in command of a highly sophisticated rotor craft, flying solo through enemy fortresses in an effort to destroy their primary defense systems. Along the way you must collect fuel and energy to power advanced Rotor features. There are a total of 18 missions, with a password system to resume play on higher levels.
