Rock N Roll

CHEATS

Type "RAINBOW ARTS" for your name. Then you can use these keys:

'F3'-----------to get 1 extra pickaxe
'F4'----------to get 1 extra speed up
'F5'--------------------to get spikes
'F6'---------------to get some armour
'F7'--------------to get 1 extra bomb
'F8'---------to get 1 extra parachute
'F9'--to get an increase in map depth
'F10'---------to go to the next level

To warp level enter the following: (level number), xx, four digits when added together equal the level number, xx, and the level number backwards. example for warping to level 12: "12 xx 3333 xx 21".

TIPS

To hear the soundtrack, type "COUNTRY".

Find the special hole on level 1 at the end of a deadend corridor. It's surounded by 3 left pointing arrows. Purchase a pick and use it on the hole. Fall down the hole and press the LEFT MOUSE BUTTON. This will warp you to level 33.

Find a level which has a huge rectangular array of eggs (I forget which level it was) and squash all the eggs on the edges in a clockwise order. You are awarded a bonus.

Find a level with a 3*3 array of holes (I forget which level it was) and three picks. Use the picks on the middle row of the holes. You are awarded a bonus.
