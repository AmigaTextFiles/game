Rampage

Produced by: Activision
Distributed by: Activision

DESCRIPTION

Smash and trash. Go ahead get it out of your system, loose your temper, smash a skyscraper, trash a city, have an office building for lunch. Go on a rampage. Includes 1-or2 player options, choose any of three mischievous monsters to control, 147 different cities to trash. Super colorful graphics and arcade-style animation.

TIPS

If you're on the last building and a cloud of dust appears under it punch in any direction and keep your finger on the fire button until the next screen is displayed, where upon you will be able to climb up an invisible building which is in the same place as the last building on the previous screen.
