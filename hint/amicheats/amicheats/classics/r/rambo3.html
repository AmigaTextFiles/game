DESCRIPTION

Get Rambo on your team! As Rambo, the world's most popular and recognizable hero, you stage a daring rescue of Colonel Trauman. Soviet commandos are holding him captive 30 miles over the Afghanistan border. The mission: Get in. Get out. Meanwhile saving the Colonel, bombing the Soviet compound, and using hijacked tanks and choppers to make a run for the border.

CHEATS

On the high score table, enter "RENEGADE" for your name. Press '1'-'3' to start at the corresponding level.

GENERAL HINTS

  • When inside the 2nd building, wear your IR goggles.
  • Don't walk in front of soldiers, since it will trigger an alarm and about 8 soldiers will jump you!
  • If you enter a dark room, use the glow tube.
  • Never use a gun without a silencer.
  • Turn off the electric door with switch located several rooms away.
  • The mine detector won't work without the generator.
  • The IR goggles won't work without a battery.
  • Watch out for ir beams, since they also trigger alarms.
