Champions Of Krynn

Produced by: Stategic Simulations, Inc.
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

Champions Of Krynn is the first AD&D fantasy role-playing computer game to be set in TSR, Inc.'s most popular AD&D game world, the DRAGONLACE world. The player guides a party of characters (up to six player characters and two non-player characters) through a web of intrigue and combat. Player characters include kenders and Knights of Solamnia as well as most races and classes of characters from the best-selling DRAGONLACE novels - Tanis, tasselhoff, and Caramon - as they struggle to overcome the forces of evil. New features include five different difficulty levels, moons with phases, and gods that grant special abilities to their clerics. A special four color poster featuring an original TSR painting is also included in the game.

CHEATS

Once you find any item you can make as many copies of it as you want.

  1. Create a dummy character.
  2. Load a saved game and transfer all the good items to the dummy.
  3. Remove the dummy character from the party.
  4. Load the dummy character back in.
  5. Transfer the items to other members.
  6. Drop the dummy character from the party.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 as many times as needed.
