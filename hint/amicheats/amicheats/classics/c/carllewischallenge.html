Carl Lewis Sports Challenge

Produced by: Psygnosis Limited
Distributed by: Psygnosis Limited

DESCRIPTION

Take charge of the training programs for your country's top 10 athletes, monitor their weekly performance, and select the top five contenders for international competition. The computer establishes a competitive environment based on your squad's training performance in javelin, 400-meter hurdles, long jump, high junp, and 100-meter sprint. Realistic digitized images of real world class athletes. Up to four player action.

CHEATS

Here's how to easily win the 100 meters: Select the speed control method, then use the mouse, joystick and keyboard all at once (you might need someone to help you) you should now finish the race in about 6 seconds.
