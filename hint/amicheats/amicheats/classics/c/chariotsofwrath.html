Chariots Of Wrath

Produced by: Impressions
Distributed by: Impressions

DESCRIPTION

While fighting for your King in far-off lands, a rival Baron has kidnapped Princess Arthena, your future Queen. Chariots of Wrath combines arcade action with the depth and challenge usually only offered by adventure or strategy gaems.

CHEATS

When you are first asked to press the fire button push the joysick forward. This will give you infinite lives.
