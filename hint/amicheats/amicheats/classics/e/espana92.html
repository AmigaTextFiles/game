DESCRIPTION

Strive for the gold in over 30 events including track and field, swimming, diving, boxing, wrestling, and fencing. As an experienced athlete, practice up to the day of the event. Or train an athlete for the games, improving stamina and fitness along the way!

TIPS

Put an athlete on training for 3 hours. Then change the date back to the 18th. Now put the athletes training down to one hour and the athlete will now be 95% fit.
