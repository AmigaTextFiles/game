DESCRIPTION

A legend is born, a supremely advanced, one-man starfighter of awesome power and destructive capabilities that, in the right hands, will wreak havoc among intergalactic transgressors - that legend is Epic. A 3-D 'epic' that reaches a milestone in polygon technology in terms of speed, graphical detail, and pure playability. A multitude of missions, from devious to manic, take you deep into the heart of this sci-fi conquest of outer space.

CHEATS

Press the 'LEFT SHIFT' and slowly type "NEM YDID EHT" and press 'RETURN'. You'll hear a sound. Now 'C' will complete your mission.

Push the joystick down and to the right and press 'ENTER' for full shields and weapons.
