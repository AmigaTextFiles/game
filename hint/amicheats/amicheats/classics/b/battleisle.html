Battle Isle

Produced by: UBI Soft
Distributed by: UBI Soft

DESCRIPTION

In a world essentially composed of islands, you are Chief of State and have complete control over the armed forces. Your goal is to attack the islands and capture the most terrain possible. To carry out your mission, you have at your disposal a port, an arsenal, an airport, train stations, factories, an army, a navy, and an air force. Each one of these forces is mobile and can accomplish the strategies of your creation. Careful, you must take into consideration the variables of the terrain: coral reefs, the height of the mountains, etc. While defending your strategic points and energy supplies, transport units and repair warehouses; demolish those of your enemy and render him helpless. Show no mercy! The time for confrontation has arrived!

TIPS

It has eluded most players on how to play against the computer. I'm sure it is buried somewhere in the long documentation file. The game defaults to a two player mode. Select options with the mouse to get to the options screen. Click on the word "first". That is the first level of the two player codes. Type in 'conra' or code for any other game level that you wish to play. Then select exit to play the game.
