CHEATS

Pause the game and type any of the following to activate the cheat mode:

"MALICEOFTHEMYRSKOIDS"
"SVANGERSKABSFOREBZGGENDEMIDDL"
"MATTRESSISAMONSTER"
"IGLSONGOMEGLEUTTQUANT"

Now use the following keys:

'1'-'9', '0', '-', '=', '\', 'BACKSPACE'--Select stage
'Q', 'W', 'E', 'R', 'T', 'Y'--------------Select stage
'UP CURSOR'--------------------Add power to life meter
'DOWN CURSOR'-----------Subtract power from life meter
'LEFT CURSOR'---------------------------Subtract speed
'RIGHT CURSOR'-------------------------------Add speed
'DEL'---------------------------------------Enter shop
