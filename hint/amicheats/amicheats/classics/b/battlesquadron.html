Battle Squadron

Produced by: Innerprise Software
Distributed by: Innerprise Software

DESCRIPTION

One and two player modes, vertical and horizontal scrolling, and chameleon enemies who become almost invisible as they mimic the background terrain. Battle Squadron delivers a unique, 3D futuristic gaming experience. Earth's forces in 2400 A.D. have traveled through space to retaliate against a devastating Barrax Empire attack. You're in command of an Earth space cruiser, dodging and darting across alien terrain, facing dozens of whirling air enemies, and challenging intense fire from ground emplacements.

CHEATS

During play, type "CASTOR" for invincibility. use 'F6'-'F10' for different weapons and 'F1'-'F5' to change the weapon's power.

On the title screen, type "ELECTRONIC" for a list of options. If this doesn't work try pressing the 'SPACEBAR".
