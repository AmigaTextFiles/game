Bards Tale III

Thief Of Fate

Produced by: Electronic Arts
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

Someone or something has sealed the city's fate with an evil so vast that a host of Paladins and an army of Archmages are outmatched. Hard times call for subtlety. Smaller is better. Sneakier is best. What the world needs now is a thief. A compelling role-playing adventure with over 13 character classes and 80 dungeon levels.

FUN

For a bit of fun tell the guard at the mad gods temple "HAMBURGER".

TIPS

You will need a wineskin or canteen to get past Valerian's tower. Learn the Gill spell from the fisherman then go to the lake behind his hut, go in to the lake to get to the Crystal Palace, then find the room with the Elixir of Life in it. Put water on the acorn by the tower, then a tree will grow, lifting the stone slab and providing you with a means of getting the Nightlance.
