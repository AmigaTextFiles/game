Battle Command

Produced by: Ocean of America
Distributed by: Ocean of America

DESCRIPTION

An arcade strategy game where the North/South divide is a life-threatening issue, the Ultra War. Ten years on, the latest phase of this siege has culminated in a World War I situation arising, and a stand-off between armies massed over a long dug-in battle front. You must succeed where other elite troops have failed, and strike a blow for freedom against the oppressive Southern Forces. Brains and priceless military hardware are your only protection. Over 15 missions, four Weapon Pods to stockpile with lethal devices, and a detailed manual included.

CHEATS

While you are playing, type in "CASTOR". The screen will flash green and you will be invincible. You can also change your weapons by pressing 'F6', 'F7', 'F8', 'F9', or 'F10' and you can change to power of your selected weapon by pressing 'F1', 'F2', 'F3', 'F4', or 'F5'.
