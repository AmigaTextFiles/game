Back To The Future III

Produced by: GameTek
Distributed by: GameTek

DESCRIPTION

Back to the Future III offers progressively difficult action set in the Old West. Each level is inspired by famous scenes from the movie, so you may have the edge if you've seen it. As players assume the roles of Marty McFly and Doc Brown at various stages of the game, it's up to them to return everyone safely to their own time. But Buford 'Mad Dog' Tannen isn't going to make it easy. Gamers encounter a buckboard chase, shooting gallery, pie throwers, and a harrowing train chase. Players assume the role of Marty and must balance their way along the top of a speeding train collecting speed logs and avoiding Buford's gang.
