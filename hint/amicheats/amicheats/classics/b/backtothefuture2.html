Back To The Future II

Produced by: GameTek
Distributed by: GameTek

DESCRIPTION

Combines five adventures. Three arcade action arenas plus two logic puzzle levels, that challenge your intellect as well as your reflexes. In order to survive Biff and the boys, Marty needs to grab as many weapons and energy boosters as possible. Marty and Doc venture through famous scenes from the hit movie like the Hill Valley Hoverboard fiasco and the Enchantment Under The Sea dance. Travel through time, via your Delorean, to 1955, 1985, and 2015. Encounter all your favorite characters including Doc, Jennifer, Biff, and Griff.

CHEATS

Pause the game and type "THEONLYNEATTHINGTODO". Press 'SHIFT Z' to skip to the next level. May need spaces after each word.
