Dawn Patrol

TIP

You don't have to be a student of aviation history to notice that WW1 aircraft didn't come equipped with either air-brakes or wheel-brakes. So the following key commands are obviously hang overs from previous Rowan flight sims which the programmers either forgot or couldn't be bothered to remove.

To make use of them just press:

'B' --- Air-brakes
'W' - Wheel-brakes
