Dojo Dan

Produced by: Europress Software
Distributed by: Europress Software

DESCRIPTION

Fast-action game based on cutesy Japanese teen-warrior. In mythical land of Banzaari, Dojo Dan takes on the evil warlord Valrog and his hideous hordes of mutants to rid his beautiful world of these tyrants. 20 grueling stages of exciting action push even experienced game players to the limits. Awesome atmosphere music, up to 160 colors on screen at once, and the smoothest multi-directional scrolling.

CHEATS

'F8'--------------Completes level
'F9'--------Completes the section
