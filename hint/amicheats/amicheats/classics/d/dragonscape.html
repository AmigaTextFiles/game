Dragonscape

Produced by: Free Spirit Software, Inc
Distributed by: Free Spirit Software, Inc

DESCRIPTION

Dragonscape is the wackiest, fastest, toughest, most addictive fantasy shoot-'em-up to appear on the Amiga. Featuring 64-way scrolling, four multi-screen levels, a full musical soundtrack and 100 types of opponents, Dragonscape is fast, furious fun.

CHEATS

Press the down cursor key to skip levels, or try 'ALT' + 'CURSOR DOWN'.

During level 1, press 'TAB' and '2' (the 2 being on numeric pad). You will be transported to Technocity. Do it again, and you will be sent to Iceland.
