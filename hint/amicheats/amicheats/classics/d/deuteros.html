Deuteros

CHEATS

Go to the surface or the orbital stores room, hold down 'Shift' and press 'C'. The screen should turn green. Now press 'Shift' and 'C' again, and the game will resume as normal. Now click on the master control icon and go to the stock screen. You will appear to have one of every item, when in fact you have an infinite supply of everything. Orbital space stations can now be built with one frame section.
