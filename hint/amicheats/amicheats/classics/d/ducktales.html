DESCRIPTION

In six different games, at three ability levels, you will search for the world's rarest treasures in a variety of exotic places. One of the first things to learn, however, is how to keep and send secrets by using secret messages. The control center in Scrooge's office is protected by Gyro Gearloose's Super Anti-Spy Security System, preventing Glomgold's henchmen, the Beagle boys, from stealing Scrooge's business secrets. The map of Scrooge's worldwide operations can be seen by using the Secret Code Sheet to decode the control center's secret password.
