Dark Castle

Produced by: Three-Sixty, Inc.
Distributed by: Three-Sixty, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Conquer numerous perils and defeat the Black Knight in the Castle of Doom. Search the castle's halls; but watch out for the bats, rats, vultures, mutants, magic brooms, and whip and rock wielding henchmen. Fourteen levels of increasing difficultly. Animated colour graphics and digitized sound effects.

CHEATS

The first thing you do is go to the shield room. Once there press 'W' (up) and 'S' (down). Keep them both pressed. This will cause your hero to go up and down between the two screens. After half an hour or so you will have about 25 lives, this is the only screen you can do it on.
