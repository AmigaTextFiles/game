Dragon's Lair 3

The Curse Of Mordread

Produced by: ReadySoft Inc.
Distributed by: ReadySoft Inc.

DESCRIPTION

After destroying the Evil Wizard Mordoc in his bid to marry to your beloved Princess Daphne in Dragon's Lair II: Time Warp, now his sister the Evil Witch Moredread, is demanding revenge. You as Dirk the Daring, must test your skills as you are thrust into a frantic quest through time to save Daphne and the Children before they are trapped in the Vortex of Eternity.

CHEATS

Type "TIME0" (that's a zero at the end) on the title screen. Then press fire to begin the non-existent game. Also works during play.
