Denaris

Produced by: Hard Wired
Distributed by: Gold Disk, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Throughout time, man has been a victim of his own intelligence and his insatiable thirst for knowledge. The planet Denaris is a horrifying example. Scientists created Machines which became so sophisticated they no longer depended on man, so began a rule of tyranny. The Denarian people tried in vain to destroy the Machines with a mighty ballistic missile, but from the ashes of the nuclear fires the Machines rose up more powerful than ever. Trapped in an underground stronghold, now the Denarians only hope for freedom is the DS-H75 Eagle Fighter, an ultra refined space glider. Control the Eagle Fighter in this epic crusade against evil... and win freedom at last for Denaris.

CHEATS

To enter the Training Mode, simply press 'Z' after game select. Now plug the mouse in port 2 and hold down the right mouse button while it loads.

For infinite lives, when the 'insert data disc' prompt appears, hold down 'Z', plug the mouse into port 2, and hold down 'RIGHT MOUSE' while it loads.
