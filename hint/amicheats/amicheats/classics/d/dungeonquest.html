Dungeon Quest

Produced by: Image Tech
Distributed by: Image Tech

DESCRIPTION

A graphic adventure featuring state-of-the-art pictures and sounds. Follow your journey through dark castles and forests.

TIPS

To get into the castle, collect the rocks from the first screen after leaving the ferry by typing "SEARCH" then "GET ROCKS". When you get to the castles moat and see the drawbridge, type "CHUCK ROCK" and the drawbridge will open.
