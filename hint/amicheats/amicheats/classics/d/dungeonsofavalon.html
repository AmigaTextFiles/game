Dungeons Of Avalon 1 & 2

CHEATS

Go to the adventures guild, and load in one of the characters given at the start (let's say you chose amber). Create five new characters, of any race or type. Make sure that all five people have at least 130 gold pieces. Go to the weapon shop, and pool all the gold, giving it to amber. Go back to the adventurers guild and remove all players but amber. Now load in the other five characters that are pre-made at the start of the game. You can now start your quest with somewhere in the region of 1000 gold pieces - unless you kit everyone up at the weapon store, train, etc. You can repeat this as many times as you want each go, giving you in effect infinite money.
