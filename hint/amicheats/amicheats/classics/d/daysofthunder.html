Days Of Thunder

Produced by: Software Toolworks, The
Distributed by: Software Toolworks, The

DESCRIPTION

Better buckle up, you're about to experience all the engine-roaring, tire-screeching stock car action of the movie Days of Thunder. This heart-pounding adventure features the challenge of eight incredible races on a variety of totally hot tracks. Choose a car, then customize it for maximum speed and handling on the course.

CHEATS

Press 'P' to pause the game and type "COMEFLYWITHME". The screen should flash. Now press 'P' again to unpause the game. This will let you fly. Pull back on the joystick and soar into the air. Use the fire button to move forward and the function keys to get a view of yourself flying through the sky.
