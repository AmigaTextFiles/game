Darkman

Produced by: Ocean of America
Distributed by: Ocean

DESCRIPTION

Once he had a normal life, a beautifil girlfriend, and a brilliant medical career - creating synthetic skin for accident victims. Then he became the victim himself, brutally attacked by sadistic criminals - his face and body burned beyond recognition. Now Darkman walks the night, searching for the woman he loves. A man who looks like a monster, he hopes to salvage his scorched flesh - and have revenge on those who destroyed his life.

CHEATS

During play type "MEA CULPA" and use the following keys:

'I'--------------------------------------Invincibility
'Z'------------------------Kills enemies on the screen
'D'-------------------------Gives darkman the disguise
'P'---------------------------------Refills life meter
'R'----------------------------------------------?????
'SPACE'---------------Bypass photo session w/ disguise
'1'-'6'----------------Skip to the corresponding level
'F1'-'F7'--------------Skip to the corresponding level
'F8'--------------------------------Skip to next level
'J'---Move darkman anywhere on the level with joystick
('J' can be toggled on/off)
