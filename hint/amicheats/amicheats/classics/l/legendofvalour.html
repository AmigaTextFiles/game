Legends Of Valour

Produced by: Strategic Simulations, Inc.
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

Fantasy becomes reality in Legends of Valour, a single-character graphic adventure from industry veteran Strategic Simulations, Inc. Set in the mythical city of Mittledorf, valour vanquishes its opposition with the smoothest scrolling three-dimensional graphics ever seen in a fantasy role playing game. Isolated inside the fortified medieval town, players create their own character by choosing their appearance, occupation, and lifestyle. Personality traits such as strenghts, memory, manipulation, health, and appeal vary with the character's race. After character generation, the player experiences over 45 exciting quests, each with its own mysteries, plot, and potential outcomes. The non-linear gameplay enables the player to explore the 28 square miles of town in any direction, including numerous shops, taverns, hostles, temples, and guilds.

TIPS

Find some treasure and pick it up, then find a person. Throw the treasure at the person. Now he (or she) will drop a bag of money. And remember that the persons with the most money are often the women.
