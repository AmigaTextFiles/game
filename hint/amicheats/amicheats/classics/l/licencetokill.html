Licence To Kill

CHEATS

Use 'F8' as a level skipper. This might only work on some of the early released versions.

TIPS

LEVEL 1

Destroy the escaping jeep as soon as possible. To do this stay in line with it and point the nose down by accelerating.

Remember to stay on the road, otherwise Sanchez's men will get you.

LEVEL 2

Stay away from the open door by taking cover close to the walls.

Running towards the enemy can make them run away, but dosen't get rid of them altogether.

Shooting the oil drums conserves bullets, but keep well back, because they destroy everything in their vicinity.

LEVEL 3

To grab the tail of Sanchez's plane, keep away from the clouds and press the fire button to attach the tow cable. Don't do this until Bond is close enough, but make sure you fire in advance to allow reaction time.

LEVEL 4

Stay under water when boats and snipers are around. Keep a full tank of oxygen and don't surface unless divers appear. Try to get a harpoon gun by stabbing one of the divers, and save it for the arrival of the seaplane, fire the harpoon at it and climb up the cable.

LEVEL 5

When you're inside the truck, you'll be under attack from other vehicles.

Trucks can be destroyed by ramming them repeatadly into the roadside. After a little of this treatment, the trailer is released. leaving the rig to do the damage, and this must be knocked out too.

Watch out for jeeps, because they carry missiles. Avoid their firepower by staying out of line, and destroy them by driving over them.
