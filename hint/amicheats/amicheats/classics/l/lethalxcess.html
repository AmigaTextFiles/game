Lethal Xcess

CHEATS

On the main title screen (the selection screen) type "COKE". When the screen flashed blue you are in the cheat mode. Now use the following keys during play:

'F1'----For triangle | 'F7'------For seeker
'F2'------For drones | 'F8'------For hunter
'F3'--For alienwiper | 'F9'------For shield
'F4'-----For blaster | 'F10'--Invincibility
'F5'-------For laser | 'V'------Slow motion
'F6'---For formation | 'L'--------Add lives

Also on the title screen type any of the following:

"FAST"-----------Increase ship speed
"AUTO"--------------Engages autofire
"TWIN"--Gives you two ships onscreen
"EXIT"---------------Resets computer
"QUIT"---------------Resets computer
"DEMO"--------------Starts game demo
The 'HELP' key--for rapid fire speed
