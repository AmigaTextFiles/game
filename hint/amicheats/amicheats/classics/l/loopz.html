Loopz

Produced by: Software Toolworks, The
Distributed by: Software Toolworks, The

DESCRIPTION

The most addictive game ever! It's very simple, you get pieces of different sizes and shapes. Then you put them together to form Loopz. You can move the pieces around the playfield and rotate them. The more complex the loop, the more points you score. It's so simple, in fact, most people can easily finish their first session in about five or ten hours!
