Lemmings 2

The Tribes

Produced by: Psygnosis Limited

DESCRIPTION

Guide 12 tribes of Lemmings across Lemming island on an incredible journey. The Lemmings are in trouble and only their ancient talisman and a huge amount of skills can save them in this long-awaited follow-up to the Lemmings saga. Meet the Highland Lemmings, Beach-Bum Lemmings, Circus Lemmings, and more on a quest to save their island.

CHEATS

On the menu screen, press the 'LEFT MOUSE BUTTON' in each of the four corners of the screen, starting at the top left and go in a clockwise direction. If you do it correctly you will hear lets go. Now you will be able to select any level from any tribe. But you still need to complete every level to receive the talisman.
