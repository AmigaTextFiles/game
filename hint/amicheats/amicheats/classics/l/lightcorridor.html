Light Corridor

Produced by: Titus Software Corporation
Distributed by: Titus Software Corporation

DESCRIPTION

A futuristic 3-D puzzle game based on controlling and moving objects through space and time. Travel at the speed of light and ensure the safe passage of chosen objects through the tunnels of time.

TIPS

The green diamonds are essential as these award you with an extra life. Some of the red diamonds will allow control over two bats but this is more of a hinderence as the two bats prove confusing. Avoid the blue squares as they will shrink the size of your bat.
