Magic Boy

CHEATS

On the options page hold down the first letter of the level you want to jump to and the number '1' or '2' for what part you wish to start playing. Now while pressing these keys hit the fire button to begin playing on that level. Here follows a list of all the levels:

'S' ----- Sand Land
'F' --- Future Zone
'P' - Plastic Place
'W' ----- Wet World
