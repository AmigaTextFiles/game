DESCRIPTION

Any mission, any where, any time... for a price. With this as your creed you can wind up in some pretty tough locations. Graveyard is just such a place; a small moon, orbiting a planet called Yalhoth in the Luyten System. Your team's current mission: to extract members of an extreme political group being held captive by a rival faction. With this objective in mind you battle your way through over 20 theaters of engagement, encountering a variety of adversaries and dispatching them with efficiency. Collecting weapons, scanners, manipulators and food, you and your team work your way through Graveyard, in pursuit of your objective. Will your team survive? Can you liberate the hostages? Enlist with the Hired Guns and test your skills.
