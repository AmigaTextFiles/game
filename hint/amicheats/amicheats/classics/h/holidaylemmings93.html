HINTS

  • If a Builder hits a steepish slope he will stop building and turn around.
  • On the early levels practice using the lemmings special abilities. Try completing level one without Climbers and level two without Floaters.
  • On Blizzard level two, you will need to use the Digger/Builder method to block off the lemmings.
  • On Blizzard level 14 the exit is hidden under snow in the steel boxes in the bottom left corner! Use Miners and Bashers to get there.
