Hybris

Produced by: Discovery Software
Distributed by: Discovery Software

DESCRIPTION

You're a pilot of a missile cruiser flying over treacherous enemy territory. Your skill and courage will take you from a minimal scouting ship to an ultra-powerful mega-cruiser. Face attacks from land, sea, and air as you try to earn your silver wings. As commander, you determine which power targets to strike to give your cruiser rapid fire, extra missiles, or even invincibility. Great visuals; game play moves every frame.

CHEATS

Once the game is loaded and prompts you to press fire, press the "SPACE BAR" instead. You will be able to change the speed of the enemy bullets and other stuff, with the mouse and keyboard. On the high score table, type "COMMANDER" on the high score screen (not as your name) press 'F7' to activate the super weapons 'F1-F6'. You should also be invincible. 'F10' turns cheat on/off and 'F8' advances a level. On the title screen press 'SPACE' or 'ESC' for an options menu.
