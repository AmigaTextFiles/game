Hoi

Produced by: Hollyware Entertainment
Distributed by: Hollyware Entertainment

DESCRIPTION

Hoi is an advanced game of strategy and reflexes made to challenge your arcade skills. Five levels of many obstacles, puzzles, and foes to evade and defeat. WARNING: The stroboscopic effect used in the fifth and final level could trigger EPILEPTIC seizures. If you're at risk, don't play level 5.

CHEATS

Try the following to gain access to the first four levels (the last level, level 5, is only available when you complete level 4): On the level-access screen, place the "LVL" mouse pointer in the far bottom right of the green quarter screen. The bottom "L" must be positioned in the corner with pixel perfect precision (two pixels on the pointer will be hanging over the edge to the right). Click the left mouse button, then shift the "LVL" mouse pointer to the very top left of the screen (as far as you can physically move the mouse to the top left) and click the left mouse button again. You should now have access to the first four levels. Another source says that after doing this you can also get 12 lives at any time by pressing 'F4' during the game.
