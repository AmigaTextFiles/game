James Pond II AGA

Robocod

CHEATS

At any time on the intro or the title screen, type 'O.S. FRIENDLY'. The screen will flash green to confirm the cheat has been activated. This enables the same in-game keys as the original. NOTE: you can now select from 0 to 55 in the map selection room.

'F' -- Gives robocod some wings
'P' --- Gives robocod the plane
'B' ---- Gives robocod the bath
'C' ----- Gives robocod the car
'X' --- Go straight to the exit
'S' ------------- Save position
'K' -------------- Kill robocod
'M' -------------- Map selector
'RETURN' - Invincibility on/off
'F6' -------------- 50 Hz (PAL)
'F7' ------------- 60 Hz (NTSC)
'F9' ----------- Screen effects

On the roof before the first door pick up the objects in this order:

Tap | Apple | Earth | Hammer | Cake

Pick them up in that order (the first letters spell CHEAT backwards). You are now invincible for about 15 minutes.
