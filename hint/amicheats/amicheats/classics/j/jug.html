Jug

Produced by: MichTron/Microdeal
Distributed by: MichTron/Microdeal

DESCRIPTION

You are an interactive humanoid composed of Titanium fleximetal and other organic materials. First built in 36412 AD, you were nicknamed JUG because of your barrel chested profile and the ability to transform into many different shapes. Your duty is to seek out the deadly tumor that is destroying the planet Spireaus. Trap doors enable you to move up or down a level. The planet's immune system will see JUG as a hostile and alien body. Beware of attacks from the very planet you are trying to rescue.

CHEATS

On the title screen hold down 'ESC' and click the mouse pointer on the right edge of the picture to activate the cheat mode. This will give you infinite energy and lives.
