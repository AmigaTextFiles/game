Nuclear War

Produced by: New World Computing, Inc.
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

A comical game of strategy and tactics. As a world leader, you must compete against four computer controled opponents for global supremacy. Experience propaganda blitzes, nuclear strikes, and some interesting weather anomalies.

CHEATS

Get a 50megaton plane up and then try to use a payload of 100 megatons with him on the following turn. You'll get an error message. Ignore it and click on 'nuclear war' for the next turn. After that you'll get a good stock of weaponry which should help you kill every bad dude in the world.
