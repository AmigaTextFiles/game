Nick Faldo's Championship Golf

CHEATS

On the title screen, type "MAJORTOM". You should now find an option to play a round on mars.

To get a hole in one everytime, choose to play as an amateur, then start the game. When you putt, the {A MULLIGAN} option will appear. Click on {OLD} and your shots should decrease by one. Repeat until you are on stroke one, and put the ball in.

TIPS

The tricky control system can be best mastered by following these tips. Don`t concentrate too hard on wrist snap, concentrate on the double click more (Wrist snap only adds 10% more power). Get used to less difficult clubs first, such as the three wood with it`s larger strike bar.
