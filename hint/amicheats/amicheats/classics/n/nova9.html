Nova 9

Produced by: Dynamix, division of Sierra On-Line
Distributed by: Sierra On-Line

DESCRIPTION

Continues the adventure saga of Steller 7. Gir Draxon returns, drawing the player into battle on the nine unexplored planets of the Nova 9 system. Piloting the new Raven II, the player races against time as Draxon's forces search for the Raven's hidden base. Quick thinking and a quick trigger finger are needed to survive as the player navigates mazes of energy, drops bombs, battles prehistoric beasts, and takes on Draxon's forces on nine worlds of increasing danger and difficulty. Complex 3-D worlds, explosive special effects. Supports analog joysticks, has save game feature.

CHEATS

During play hold down any of the following key combinations:

'CTRL', 'ALT', and 'HELP'--Shield recharge & Damage fix
'CTRL', 'ALT', and 'RETURN'-------Adds Lasers & Rockets
'CTRL', 'ALT', and 'CURSOR UP'---------------Level skip
