Universe 3

Produced by: Omnitrend Software, Inc.
Distributed by: Omnitrend Software, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Animated role-playing adventure game. Set in the 24th century, you lead a diplomatic mission to Earth. Features a sophisticated mouse based parser for rapid play. Also includes digitized sound effects.

SOLVE

Go West, South, South, and PICKUP bent metal bar. Go North, North, East, East, and PICKUP piece of circuit board. Go East, stay in the middle of the place and wait till the spinning asteroid shows up and JUMP on it (if you fail the first time, just wait and try again). Go East (wait), JUMP on the duct and you should be back in the place you started the game.

Here stand near the satellite dish and INSERT bent metal bar into panel, INSERT circuit board into panel, then USE panel. The terminal menu will show up. Select CONNECT CHANNEL option and GAVRIC HOMEWORLD, USE panel again, select SET FILTRATION SYSTEM and GAVRIC HOMEWORLD (should be OFF, if its ON, try again), Go North, and Stand near the ventilation shaft to the left of the screen and PUSH/PULL it. You should come out at the same planetoid as in the beginning of the game.

PICKUP mirror, repeat all the steps with the spinning asteroid and go to the room with the ventilation shaft again. Go North-West to another place, and here on the right of the screen is the doorway with 2 lasers over it. THROW mirror at the doorway and the lasers should get burned (You can do it only if the robot that is moving on the screen is outside). The Alien will tell you to go and see Silphinaa, USE door console at the northern doorway. Talk with Silphinaa until she will allow you inside. Enter and say that you don't know where you are. Read the rest of the conversation until she tells you to go upstairs. ATTACK keypad (on the door) with bent metal bar, OPEN wardrobe, and WEAR suit (its inside the wardrobe). USE arm computer on the console over the bed. USE console, select WINDOW SHUTTER option. JUMP through the window, and USE arm computer on the car. JUMP into car, INSERT key-card into console to the left of the screen. USE anti-theft keypad to the right of the screen, and enter 87764.

Ok, now you are in space, you'll have some kind of vision, where you'll see your soon-to-be pursuer Baron Kaleev, then you'll meet with Snorglat galactic trader. Talk with him using options 1, 1, he will leave, now you can use your PTV car by clicking on the console in the middle of the screen. Select NAVIGATE COURSE. Check the position of the planet Balkamos 7 (to the left of your actual position). Use LAY IN COURSE icon and select Balkanos 7 as a destination. Select DESCEND TO PLANET option and SELECT QUADRANT. Choose north-eastern quadrant. Go north, PICKUP droid, and go east, south, south, then PICKUP canister. COMBINE droid + canister, then go west, west, north, and here is your car with some nasty aliens on it. USE droid on alien. JUMP into car. Use ASCEND FROM PLANET option and use NAVIGATE COURSE option. Next use LAY IN COURSE icon to Jor-Slev 4 planet, and use DESCEND TO PLANET option. SELECT QUADRANT, and choose N-W part of the planet. Go east, PICKUP rock, and THROW rock at Alien. USE droid on blob, and you should now have Blobs in your inventory. Go back to car. JUMP into car, and use SELECT QUADRANT option. Choose S-E part of the planet. Go west, west, and talk with the alien until you have the chance to offer him blobs. Select this option, and he will give you carvite in exchange. Go east, east, and JUMP into car. Choose ASCEND FROM PLANET, NAVIGATE COURSE, LAY IN COURSE to Pfenellop Asteroid Cluster. You'll meet Snorglat again. Offer him carvite, and he will agree to take you to WheelWorld.

Now its a simple arcade part where you have to dock to Snorglat's spaceship using the left mouse button to move your car and right mouse button to catch it. Nothing hard anyway. When you arrive at WheelWorld choose any dialog with Snorglat, he will paralyse you anyway and take the carvite. JUMP into car, LAY IN COURSE to Landing Bay - REF.40e. Here you'll find a working droid. PUSH droid, and PICKUP cable. JUMP into car, LAY IN COURSE to Landing Bay - REF.1h. Go east through the open portal. Go east, north, go near dispenser, and INSERT i.d card into dispenser. Select ORDER option, you'll now have synthetic carvite in your inventory. Go west, enter through the door into the bar, you can use console to the right to play SPACE INVADERS! Talk with the guy who sits alone. Select dialog: 2, 2, 2, and you'll go and buy a bottle of Gh'Narhl brandy. COMBINE brandy with syntetic carvite. SAVE here because the game is a little bit buggy. When you now try to talk with the guy again, sometimes it gives you the 1st conversation again and you lose your carvite. So talk with the same guy again and in case its the same conversation as the one you already had, load your saved position and it should be fine. Then, he will drink brandy with carvite and fall asleep, you'll get the badge. Exit the bar, go south, and you'll find a healer being attacked by some jet-packers. While talking select dialog 3, 3, and you'll follow the jet-packers.

Here is another arcade part of the game which is also quite easy. After combat, you'll find an injuried jet-packer. Select dialog: 1, 2, 1, and you'll have the healer's hand now. Go east, north, and east again, talk with the healer. Follow him west, here you'll get attacked by baron Kaleev and his droids. Your character will run to the room with the elevator. Stand on the elevator, and it will start to go down but then blow up. USE cable on the elevator to go down. Go beetween the buildings to the east. Stand on the bridge above the rails. JUMP on the train when it shows up. In the next screen fastly JUMP on the anchion to the right of you. JUMP to the lower entrance (on the left of the screen), and go near the door. USE droid on the air duct to the left of the door. He will open the door for you. USE I.D card on the ordering terminal, and select MEKANTHALLOR GALAXY as the destination route to obtain a ticket. USE door to the left. USE ticket on the terminal. Go east, use keypad on the door to enter the ship. You'll find yourself in the lounge of big spaceship. USE lift to go to level 1. Here are 2 guys speaking about a healer aboard the ship. Talk to them. Select dialog: 3, 1. Go left, USE door console, watch the transmission for you. USE communicator, select LEVEL 3, MYRELL. Exit your room. Go east. USE lift to reach level 3. Go east, north, east, east, north, east, east. USE doorconsole, and dialog: 1, 1, 1, exit the room. Here you'll meet the assasin sent to kill you. Select dialog: 1, 2, and the explosion on the ship will knock her unconsious. PICKUP key-card from the assasin and go back to lift. Select LAUNCH BAY, JUMP into blue car 3rd from the right side. Watch Emperor King's fleet.

Then NAVIGATE COURSE, LAY IN COURSE to ANKARLON 5, DESCEND TO PLANET, and SELECT QUADRANT. Choose N-W part, go west, PULL junk to the left of the screen to get straight metal bar. COMBINE both parts of starchart. COMBINE starchart with straight metal bar. Go east, JUMP into car and fly to the mekanthallor galaxy again and then to Daarlor-Korv. Land on the S-E part of the planet, go east, east. INSERT starchart+spindle into recess. You'll get teleported to some weird place. Go east, and PICKUP the rock. LOOK at the plaque, it will show you some kind of diagram with 6 stones. Now, you have to run through all of these stones (each disables one of the laser beams) ending at the one in the entrance to this room. Standing on it, fastly throw a rock at the power gem. PICKUP the gem and run to the exit! In the next room, JUMP over the hole and run to the teleport, try again if you get caught by demons. Go west, west, and JUMP into car. Fly to Ankarlon 5 again. Land on N-W part, and go west. LOOK at various places on the giant ship until you find a terminal. INSERT metal bar into it. USE power gem on terminal. USE arm computer on terminal. Choose 2nd option, and transporter beam will show up. Go there, and have an interesting talk with an Alien of the Mekalien race. Select dialog: 2, 3, 2. He will give you a cloaking device. Go east, JUMP into car and ascend from planet. Fly to mekanthallor galaxy again, here you'll get on board of the rebellion ship. Select dialog: 2, 1, 2, 1. Use transporter pad to land on Coros. Here you'll meet 2 smugglers who will stole your car. Select dialog: 2, 2, 3 to get with it fast. Go north, and TALK to waiting man. Select dialog: 2, 1. He will lead you to the room with droids. Wait till he leaves. Go west too, then south. Speak for a while with sentinels, dialog: 2, 1. Go back, talk to the robot. Choose dialog: 1, 2 (about the sentinels), wait till he comes back. Choose dialog 2 and wait till he gets blasted. Go south, and PICKUP batteries. COMBINE power gem with cloaking device. COMBINE power gem with batteries. USE arm of computer on power gem. Go west, Baron will wait for you and try to kill you. Fortunately power gem defends you and you can get I.D pass out, Keelev. USE arm computer on power gem. Go back to the room with the 4 droids. Talk to the rightmost one, he will recognise you now as Baron Keelev. Select dialog: 2, 1, 1. He will blast a hole in the wall, and you'll enter it. SAVE here!

This is another arcade part, you'll have to run EAST, UP, EAST, EAST, UP (room with the rock, but don't kill the droid with it coz the game likes to stop sending droids after you when you kill 1 with the rock, so just keep running. Also if you save while running, most of the time after loading such a version you'll get automatically killed (another bug in the game). Go EAST, SOUTH, EAST, UP, EAST. You should be now in the room with giant lizard. Quickly, THROW I.D chip into lizard's head and JUMP on lizard's tail. Wait for the droid to come after you. He will get killed by the lizard. PICKUP batteries, COMBINE power gem with batteries. USE arm computer on power gem to turn invincible again. Go up, west, you should be in the room with security droids. PUSH the one thats got his back to you. Go north-west, and TALK to man-brute, selecting dialog: 2, 1. He will give you his armoured glove. Go west, and watch Emperor King's silly talk. Select dialog: 1, 1. Baron will get terminated by the king, select dialog: 1, 1, 1, 1...
