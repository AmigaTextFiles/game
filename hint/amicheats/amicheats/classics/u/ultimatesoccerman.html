Ultimate Soccer Manager

CHEATS

At the start of the game, enter your name in as "MAKE BELIEVE". You will now resume under the name of "Cheating Person" and have various cheat options at your disposal. They are as follows:

'+' --- Adds £100,000 to your balance (except during a match)
'G' --- Gives a goal to the last player to touch the ball
'E' --- End game and go straight to a penalty shoot-out
'F' --- Gives away a foul
'Esc' - Quit current half, leaving the score as it stands
'1' --- Ends the match instantly with a result of 1-0
'2' --- Ends the match instantly with a result of 2-0
'3' --- Ends the match instantly with a result of 3-0
'4' --- Ends the match instantly with a result of 0-1
'5' --- Ends the match instantly with a result of 0-2
'6' --- Ends the match instantly with a result of 0-3
