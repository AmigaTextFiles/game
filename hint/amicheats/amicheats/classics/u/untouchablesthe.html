Untouchables, The

Produced by: Ocean of America
Distributed by: Ocean of America

DESCRIPTION

Based on the smash-hit movie. The Untouchables virtually blasts its way onto your computer screen. The game's spectacular busts, raids, shootouts, and duels put you in control of Eliot Ness' elite squad of Mafia-busters. You'll relive the knife-edge existence of these fabled lawmen as they crusade against lawlessness and injustice through the crime-infested streets of Chicago in the 1920's. Take on the Mob as you lead the Untouchables on their most difficult mission ever. Every minute contains explosive action and intense adventure, and a thrilling denoument ends the game.

CHEATS

While playing, type "SOUTHAMPTONGAZETTE". Press 'F10' to skip levels and press 'HELP' to skip to the second half of some of the mission. Also, during play, pause the game and type any of the following to jump to different levels:

1-"BRIDE ROLLS" | 3---"KID ZAPPING"
2-"MAC N ALLEY" | 4-"A NIT IN TIME"
