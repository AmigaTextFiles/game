Sensible World Of Soccer

CHEATS

Line up to take a shot on goal. As soon as you take the shot press the 'R' key. Keep the fire button pressed and when the screen returns the players will be frozen and the ball will not be saved.

To get a free player:

Go to transfers and offer to buy a teams first striker and substitute striker. Now set the money equal to 0, and offer your main striker, all in that order. Assuming the other club accept the bid, you will keep your main striker while acquiring the other teams main striker also. This should work for all players of the same position.
