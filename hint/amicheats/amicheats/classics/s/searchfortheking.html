Search For The King

SOLVE

IN NEW YORK

Inside Les room, walk to table. Open drawer. Look inside drawer. get thermos and lunch bag.

Walk to hall. Stand at the cooler. Open thermos. Fill thermos with water.

Go to the boss's office. Ask for raise. When Stella came in, the boss will turn his eye and look at her. At this moment, get the key and walk out the room.

Walk to hall, unlock door. Walk inside the store room. Look inside tools box. Get reporter ID.

Walk to Lobby, there is a guard sleeping there. So, look dream and then get the dream of size.

Go to the Circus. Walk until you find the trailer. Knock door. Talk to Bob. Ask for job.

Now walk to elephant area, there is a pile of shit on the ground. So, start clearing them by getting the shovel. After working for a while, drop shovel and walk to the trailer. Knock door, ask Col Bob for the free ticket.

With the free ticket, you will be able to look at the various sport at the circus. Now look around until you find the gypsy wagon. Walk inside to ask about your future. Once inside, ask about fortune a few time until the girl say nothing else. Touch girl, and the girl will disappear. Touch the lizard and a card will come out of it mouth. Get the resurrection card. Look wax and then get wax.

Find Luigi in the circus. Look picture. Look difference. Ask about mustache. Give wax to him. After that get rosin.

Now find Helmut, the world smallest man. Give him the dream. Then get Helmut.

Now walk to the popcorn stall, get popcorn. Walk inside the circus, you will see three lions. Throw popcorn at lion, when the lion starts to eat the popcorn, walk to the right. You will find Fred and his assistant there. Give the rosin to Fred, after his performance, get his cape.

Walk to mailbox outside the bus station. Mail helmut.

Go to Bobbi's house. Knock door, show ID. She will then bring you inside. Sit at the couch beside her. Ask for soda. Show scarf. Spill soda on scarf. You will then be chased out of the house.

Walk to empty lots, climb fence. Get the scarf on the clothesline.

Go to circus, walk to the Test-O-Strength. Stand just below the bell, this will take you to Las Vegas.

AT LAS VEGAS

Walk right to desert, at the phone booth, answer phone. Drink water, this will give you back your sense of direction. You can then go back to the mailbox. Open mailbox, look inside mailbox, get Helmut.

Walk left to Vegas Hotel. Walk to the stairs which will take you to the hotel.

Walk inside hotel, at the counter, call Mr. Fabulous. Walk to pool, sit at the chair beside Lyla. After she went swimming, get glasses.

Walk to elevator, press button. Walk to typical room. Walk inside the bathroom, look inside sink. Get floss. Look at door. Look at sign. Walk to door at centre of screen, get sign. Reverse sign. Look sign. Place sign on door. Walk to bed, sit on bed. Walk to cart, look inside cart. When the maid walk over to tidy the bed, get the skeleton key which is in the cart.

When you have the skeleton key, you will then be able to access another room. So walk to elevator, press button. You will now find yourself in Mr Fabulous room. Walk to left screen. Look inside tub. Look drain. Tie helmut to floss. Lower helmut in drain. Helmut will then return with a receipt. Look receipt. Get floss, you will need it later.

Walk to elevator, press button. You will now be back to the ground floor. Walk left to the Dry Cleaners. Open door, walk inside. Show the receipt to Susie. Look suit. Get suit.

Now go outside of the Hotel. Thumb. A bus will come and bring you to Kingdom.

AT KINGDOM

Walk around, until your are at Red's bar. Walk inside the phone booth. Wear suit. You will need to have the sunglasses, white suit, scarf and cape for this to work. When you have changed, walk to door, open door and get inside. When you are in, dance. You will then be rewarded, ie the gate of the Kingdom is opened.

With gate opened, walk inside and move around until you have reach the king place.

At Foyer, look inside bear mouth. Lower Helmut in mouth.

Walk left to the awards room. Walk to guitar, get guitar. Look guitar. Fix guitar. Get the mike near the trophies.

Walk to dining room, look bowl. Get banana.

Walk to the King's bedroom. Open bureau. Look inside bureau. Touch mechanism. Look pole. Slide pole. Release pole, you will end up inside the kitchen.

Inside kitchen, get bread. Walk to counter. Open counter. Walk inside counter. Stand just to the right of the stove. Spread butter on bread. Les will then prepare a banana sandwich and eat it. This will make Les fatter. Now you really look like the KING!

Walk to Red's bar. Open door, get inside. Sing. Les will then be killed by the king's fans. He will ended up in heaven and found the king there. And because of his resurrection card, he is able to come back to life and take the photograph to his boss.

Well done, you have finally found the KING.
