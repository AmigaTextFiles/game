"MBARIVIDISOCCAFFARIMBARI"--To play as Shadow fighter
"PARAPONZIPONZIPO"------------------To play as Pupazz
"TIRIKAKKU"----------------------For infinite credits

"EBBRAVOSCECCU"----To win the current round
"CUNIGGHIUACCURRENNU"--To Speed up the game
"SURICIMONCU"--------------For normal speed
