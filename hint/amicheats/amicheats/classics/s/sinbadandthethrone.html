Sinbad And The Throne Of The Falcon

Produced by: Cinemaware Corporation
Distributed by: Cinemaware Corporation

DESCRIPTION

You are Sinbad the Sailor, battling dark and mysterious forces in an epic seafaring quest. Get ready for pirates and genies, romance and heartbreak, comedy and tragedy, set in a fantastic universe that begs to be explored. Steel yourself for the ultimate challenge to your skills as swordsman, leader and navigator. The task is formidable, but the lovely princess has begged for your aid.

Game features a unique blend of role playing and strategy, combined with dazzling arcade-style sequences.

CHEATS

As you are about to die select the close icon, then continue. You'll be as good as new and back at the map.
