DESCRIPTION

Traveling through time you do battle in one of the most destructive combat systems available, the Walker. Jumping from one historical battlefield to the next you blast everything in sight as wave after wave of combatants fall under your twin gattling cannons. Decimating your foes, the Walker works toward its ultimate goal: the destruction of your present day enemy. With a unique control system that allows you total control of the Walkers movements and attack capabilities, uou can take your Walker to new heights in destructive power.
