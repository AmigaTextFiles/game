Windwalker

Produced by: Origin Systems Inc.
Distributed by: Origin Systems Inc.

DESCRIPTION

Lightening-fast martial arts action combined with extensive role-playing. Encounter a variety of colorful characters in palaces, monasteries, pagodas and countryside located throughout multiple regions. Explore the culture, personalities and events of the ancient Orient. Animated combat sequences with authentically depicted gymnastic and acrobatic movements. Features hundreds of characters including merchants, alchemists, villagers and farmers.

CHEATS

Type in "BADGER" if the WindWalker ask you for the SECOND order of earth, and so forth. NOTE: You don't need the first order.
