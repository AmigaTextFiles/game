War In The Gulf

Produced by: ReadySoft, Inc.
Distributed by: ReadySoft, Inc.

DESCRIPTION

War in the Gulf pits a crack unit of M1 tanks against the invading Iraqi forces in northern Kuwait. Utilising the same technology as Team Yankee, War in the Gulf is a game of immense tension and hectic action set against a backdrop of hundreds of burning oil wells in the oilfields near the Kuwaiti border.
