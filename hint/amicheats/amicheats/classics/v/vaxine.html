Vaxine

Produced by: U.S. Gold
Distributed by: Accolade

DESCRIPTION

Fight your very own battle against the fortress of biological destruction using your very own Vaxine. Eliminate the invading virus cells before they can join together to form lethal viral strings that are capable of choking the life from your key body cells. Go on the attack with your own color-coded antibodies, or use your DNA strands to strangle large clusters of alien invaders before they reach your brain and numb your senses forever.

CHEATS

While playing, type "WILDEBEESTE" or it could be "WILDEBEAST" and use the following keys:

'F1'----------------Advance one level
'F2'-------------------Back one level
'F3'---------------Advance ten levels
'F4'---------------Retreat ten levels
'F10--Advance one level and get bonus
