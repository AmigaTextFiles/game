DESCRIPTION

Vektor Storm makes you commander of a small space exploration fleet that accidently falls through a black hole. On the other side, reality is turned upside down as you discover an alternative, fragmented universe where truth is false and right is wrong. Even the very laws of physics have changed. Lurking in the infinite darkness, an army of nasty alien life forms waits to destroy you and your fleet. To return to this universe, you must first meet the challenge set before you by your alien adversaries - beat them at their own game. Your four ships must fight against and arsenal of intellegent weapons on a series of battlegrids. The rules are simple, the stakes are life and death itself.
