Viking Child

Produced by: GameTek
Distributed by: GameTek

DESCRIPTION

Introducing the Prophecy series, a trilogy of unique arcade action-style adventures. The hero Brian is featured in each volume during a seperate stage of his life, starting with Brian as a child in the first release, Viking Child. Brian learns of his heroic destiny from the Wizard Odin, and sets out to rescue his family from the Evil God Loki. But first he must do battle with hundreds of strange villains, and defeat the eight apprentices of darkness. Sixteen challenging levels.
