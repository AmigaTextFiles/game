Yosemite Sam

CHEATS

At the beginning, enter your name in as "BUGS" for infinite energy. Pressing 'F1' will also now take you into a sprite editor which allows you to create, load and save your very own sprite. There are also several on the disk to choose from, they include:

1 - ALF
2 - Superman
3 - A nude woman
4 - Arnold Schwartzenegger
