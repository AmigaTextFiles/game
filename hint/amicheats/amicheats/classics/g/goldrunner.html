DESCRIPTION

Thr first installment of the Goldrunner series. Join in as humankind prepares to leave earth and enter the Golden Age. Can you become the fabled Goldrunner in the battle for the Ring-Worlds of Triton?

CHEATS

During play press 'I' to skip to the bonus level and press 'U' on the bonus level to go to the next level. By continually pressing 'I' and 'U' it is possible to skip all the levels.

Hold 'F5' for a couple of seconds and you will become indestructable.

Press 'F2', 'F5', 'F4', 'F3' one at a time for infinite everything.

Start a one player game and crash into the first building. Now hold down 'F5' until the status screen appears. You should now be able to fly through everything!

Type "EASYMODE" on the high-score table. From now on, 'F9' skips levels and makes you invulnerable. 'F8' turns off the cheat mode.
