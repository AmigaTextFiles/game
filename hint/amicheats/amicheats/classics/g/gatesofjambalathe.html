Gates Of Jambala, The

CHEATS

On the title screen type "YICKE YACKE HIHNERKACKE" (with spaces), hold down the left shift button, hold down "1", release "1", release left shift. The title should turn to blue. Go to the game. Keys 1 to 7 take you to the levels, F1 to F5 select weapons and "G" gives you gold. Check out the other function buttons as well.
