Global Effect

Produced by: Millenium
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

Should you take a turn saving the Earth? You may choose to save, rule, or create a world. Spread before a massive game area of 64,000 seperate locations, Global Effect simulates the interactions of economical, industrial, environmental, and military factors in managing world balance. You need to take into account other players and the broader backdrop of environmental balance. Most options available to the governments of the world will be available to you, the leader of a task force sent on government missions to secure distant worlds for the growing galactic civilization.

CHEATS

Type "PLENTY MORE WHERE THAT CAME FROM". Go with the 'Snooze Button' in CLI. In the Pull-up-menu 'GLOBAL EFFECT' you can now selected 'Zzzz' and all your troubles will be over.
