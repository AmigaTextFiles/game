Genghis Khan

Produced by: Koei Corporation
Distributed by: Koei Corporation

DESCRIPTION

Genghis Khan catapults the player into an era of world conquest and domination. There are two seperate scenarios - Mongol Conquest and World Conquest - each offering a game system that virtually never puts the player in the same position twice, allowing for endless hours of absorbing game play. As a player you attempt to build an empire. Keep it by making your population happy, your treasury full, your subordinates loyal, and your enemies at bay. The human side of empire building has not been forgotton. Finding the right way to approach a hostile empress is just as important as training troops in battle. Do not let your guard down, and be ready for a host of problems.

TIPS

If you get a land that isn't connected with an enemy, move everything to your home country. The land will be safe, but if another leader captures a nearby land, make sure you put something back in or he will attack on his next turn. Don't move all your forces into your command unit, or an enemy commander will wait till he is down to his last few men and challenge you to a one on one fight. If you loose he will get half your command unit.
