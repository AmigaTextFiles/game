CHEATS

To give your troops unlimited action points.

Lets say you have 3 action points left, and you are next to an enemy. Attack the enemy unit using the "Attack" button on the troop menu, then after the battle starts, click "Retreat" quickly. You now 10 action points! You can repeat this as often as you like. It's very useful for moving from Judukan to Baal in one turn!
