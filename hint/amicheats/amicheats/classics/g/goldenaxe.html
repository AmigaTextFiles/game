Golden Axe

Produced by: Electronic Arts
Distributed by: Electronic Arts

DESCRIPTION

The Death Adder is tyrannizing the people of Yuria. Only three heroes have the strength to stop the ruthless tyrant and regain the throne. Tyris-Flaire, a beautiful and powerful amazon is empowered by a special fire magic. Gilus-Thunderhead rumbles and tumbles to the scene with his astonishing combination of lighting power and gymnastic skills. And the Axe Battler has sharpened his giant sword for battle. You must choose which challenger to send into the dreaded path of the Death Adder. Your weapons range from razor-sharp swords to curtains of fire. You can even joust with your foes. Remember, one dose of magic can wipe away an acre of wvil.

CHEATS

Play in one player mode but with two joysticks. When you die, press 'fire' on the other joystick and you should receive three more lives.
