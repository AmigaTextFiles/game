Alien Breed CD32

Special Edition

Produced by: Team 17 Software
Distributed by: Team 17 Software

DESCRIPTION

Alien Breed is another classic Team 17 title which is not to be missed. Budding space mercenaries can stop chomping at the bit and kick butt with this incredible experience. Completing the line up of this tremendous CD is Quack, a cute platform game for 1 or 2 players which is dangerously playable. All games remain more or less unchanged from their original Amiga releases, although they have been adapted to make use of the CD32 gamepad and all it's buttons.
