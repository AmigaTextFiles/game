Apidya

CHEATS

Pause the game and type "ULRDABBA" or "ULMDABBA" for full weaponry (this only works once, if you type it again you'll lose a life).

On the title screen type one of the following:

STAGE 2 - "MISSHONEYBEE"
STAGE 3 - "DEPUTYOFLOVE"
STAGE 4 - "HASTALAVISTA"
STAGE 5 - "SNEAKPREVIEW"
FINALE --- "SHOWCREDITS"

Hit return after you type "SHOWCREDITS".

When you press 'HELP' and 'DEL' at the same time the on-screen action will slow down approximately 50%.
