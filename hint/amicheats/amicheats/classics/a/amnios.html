DESCRIPTION

Amnios is a Symbiotic Construct of Machine-Mind and Biology. Each element is a disparate cell in an organism sharing one consciousness and purpose: to eradicate the malignancy that threatens all of life. Controlling a tiny ship, you fight your way through each of ten deadly living worlds trying to either destroy the planet's vital organs or rescue a given number of encapsulated humoniods.
