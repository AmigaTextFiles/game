CHEATS

During play type "CHEATERCHEATERWIMP" for $50,000,000.

Press 'CAPS LOCK' then type in "CHEATERCHEATERWIMP". After two seconds your funds go up by $1 million and all the land you own is filled with building blocks. You can repeat this as many times as you like.

FUN

Try clicking on the stadium once it has been built, you should see an amusing message.

October 1st/2nd ----- Birds migrate south
December 24th/25th -- Santa rides his sled from north to south (10pm-1am)
