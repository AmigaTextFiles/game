Arkanoid

Produced by: Discovery Software
Distributed by: Discovery Software

DESCRIPTION

Brick and paddle arcade game. Digital stereo sound, 66 levels of play. Features animation and digital quality sound in a game that tests your reflexes and challenges your split-second decision-making skills. Deflect the rebounding energy ball to destroy mesmerizing brick formations. Each level is tougher than the last.

CHEATS

Press the 'SPACEBAR' to pause the game, and type "DSIMAGIC" or type "MINIMAGIC". A yellow capsule "DS" will fall. Catch it. Then use the following keys to drop the corresponding capsule:

'B'--Opens gate to next level
'C'-------Lets you catch ball
'D'---Splits into three balls
'E'----Give you a larger vaus
'F'---Warps to the last level
'L'---------Gives you a laser
'P'-----------------Extra man
'S'-----------Slows down ball
'ENTER'----------Skips levels

In addition, pressing 'F' will send you to the last level to fight DOH. Be sure to give yourself lots of lives first.

You can skip any level by using ENTER on the keypad.

To access the extra 33 levels (assuming you have that version), press F3 for a one player game and F4 for a two player game (instead of F1 or F2).
