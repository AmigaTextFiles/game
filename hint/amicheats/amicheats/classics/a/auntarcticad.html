Auntarctic Adventure

Produced by: Mindware Int.
Distributed by: Mindware Int.

DESCRIPTION

Multi-level arcade game with challenges and surprises for the experienced gamer. Charlie the Chimp has talked his good friend Penguin Pete into helping him rescue his Aunt, who has been kidnapped by Big Borus, the evil ringmaster, who has forced her to work in his circus in the Arctic. You must battle your way through each of 50 levles. Full stereo sound effects and music, 5400 full-color screens.

CHEATS

Start the game and when it says {LEVEL 01} in the middle of the screen type "JESSICA". This will give you ten extra guys. You can type this in twice or maybe three times and you will get ten extra guys added to your current stock each time. This can also be done at the start of any level.
