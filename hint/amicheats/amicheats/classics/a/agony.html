DESCRIPTION

From the programmers of Unreal comes a new adventure full of magic, mystery, and mayhem. As Leffly, heir to the secret of universal energy, you will fight your way through six agonizing levels of magical mayhem. Confront your chilling challenges in human form, take flight as an arcane owl, or battle your enemies in the form of a gruesome ghost.

CHEATS

On the main title screen (when the piano music is playing), type "FANTASY" to activate the cheat. Now use the following keys during play:

'F1' ------ Add bottom sword
'F2' ------- Add upper sword
'F3' - Increase weapon power
'F4' ---- Get one extra life (Must be pressed 3 times)
'F5' ------------------- ???
'Return' --- advance a level
