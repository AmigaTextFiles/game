Aquaventura

Produced by: Psygnosis Limited
Distributed by: Psygnosis Limited

DESCRIPTION

Aliens take over Earth and destroy your mother ship. As the only surviving human, you return in your Aquacraft to relentlessly attack the evil Spruden invaders. Battle your way through all their defenses until you reach their Enemy Weapons Depot.

Ray traced action movie introduction, 10 increasingly deadly levels of non-stop action with 3-D sprites, real-time vector graphics, and digital stereo sound.

CHEATS

On the option screen, hold down 'CTRL' and 'F10' then press fire. Now use any of the following keys during play.

'L'-----------------Skips levels
'T'--Skips to the tunnel section
