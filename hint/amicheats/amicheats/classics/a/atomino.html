Atomino

Produced by: Psygnosis Limited
Distributed by: Psygnosis Limited

DESCRIPTION

A brain teasing game, featuring an orchestrated stereo soundtrack, and offering crisp graphics and intuitive gameplay. In this innovative hybrid between dominoes and an electronic brain-teaser, you play the role of a mad-chemist. Your objective is to form molecules from the atoms which spontaneously generate in your test tube. However, the road to this deceptively simple goal is littered with increasingly difficult restrictions, including specifications on the finished molecule's size, the shape of your creations, and the omnipresent ticking of the clock. Over 60,000 levels.
