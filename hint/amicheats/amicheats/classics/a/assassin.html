Assassin

Produced by: Team 17
Distributed by: Team 17

DESCRIPTION

Foul and disgusting Midan, tyrant of the highest order, lies hidden deep within his underground complex. The security forces have lowered you, the Assassin, deep behind enemy lines to find the source of Midan's strength and destroy it. Face overwhelming odds that include Midan's loyal forces, vicious dogs, rogue machines, traps, and more on the mission of a lifetime!

CHEATS

On the first level, climb the left side of the first tree you come to, climb to the very top. Now press 'P' to pause the game and type "ACEVIEWFROMUPHEREMATE" and the screens border will flash. Unpause the game and use the following keys:

'1-6'---Go to corresponding level with infinite energy
'W'-------------Gives you maximum weapon configuration
'E'----Takes you straight to the end of level gaurdian
'C'--------------------------Grants infinite continues

Or, on the high score table, enter one of the following:

"ASSASSIN"- | "THE ONE THE ONLY" | "SUPERFROG" | "ALIEN BREED"
"PROJECT X" | "PSIONIC SYSTEMS"

Enter "MIDAN" to continue from where you left off.
