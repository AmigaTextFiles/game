Amazing Spiderman

Produced by: MicroProse Software
Distributed by: MicroProse Software

DESCRIPTION

Mysterio, the master criminal and illusionist, has kidnapped Peter Parker's wife, Mary Jane. To rescue her, Peter Parker calls on his alter ego, the Amazing Spider Man. As the famous web-slinger, you'll invade Mysterio's lair to battle the super-villain's robotic guards, tricks, and traps.

CHEATS

Type your name as "GENERIC" on the high score table. When you press 'HELP' during the game your energy will replennish.
