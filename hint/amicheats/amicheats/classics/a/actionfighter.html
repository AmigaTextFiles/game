Action Fighter

Produced by: Sega
Distributed by: MindScape

DESCRIPTIION

You are Super Rider, assigned to carry out five dangerous air and ground battles in the Sega arcade game. You're riding a motorcycle, but if you find the right parts along the road it transforms into a supercharged sports cars or state of the art airplane.

CHEATS

When asked to enter your name, type "ZBACKDOOR" for your name. You will now have infinite lives and energy.
