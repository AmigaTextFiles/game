
        While playing, type "WILDEBEESTE" or it could be "WILDEBEAST" and
        use the following keys:

                'F1'__________________________Advance one level.

                'F3'_________________________Advance ten levels.
                'F4'_________________________Retreat ten levels.
                'F10'___________Advance one level and get bonus.
