

 Cheat Mode:

 These codes are selected on the options screen at the beginning of the
 game.
 Place the mouse cursor in a corner of the screen and then press the left
 mouse button, [Ctrl] and [Alt] all together to access the cheat mode.
 Now by clicking a second time in any of the corners you will receive the
 following:

 Top left: Gives you a fully loaded super train with everything you need to win
 Top right: As above but more of a challenge with a super strong enemy
 Bottom left: Provides you with a supertrain and all the objects and the scenario to win
 Bottom right: Gives you victory
