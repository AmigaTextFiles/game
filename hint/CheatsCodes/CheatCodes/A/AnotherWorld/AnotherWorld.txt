

 Level Codes:

 At any time just press [C] and enter any of these codes:

 Code   Start

 EDJI: In the lake.
 HICI: In the cage.
 FLLD: In the sewers.
 LIBC: 1st recharger.
 CCAL: In the caves.
 EDIL: End of the cave.
 FADK: T-shaped rock.
 KCIJ: Temple entrance.
 ICAH: By the pool.
 FIEI: Powerline.
 LALD: Blast run.
 LFEK: Female sanctum.


 Playing Tips:

 It is worth remembering after the blast run in the arena the directions
 for pressing the correct buttons are:

 Fire, down, fire, left 2, down, fire, up 3, and fire.
 You will now be launched into the female sanctum.
