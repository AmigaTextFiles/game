        '1'________________Baseball bat.    'HELP'______Jump to next level.
        '2'___________________Golf club.    'I'____________Invulnaribility.
        '3'______________Tennis racquet.    'L'__________Get an extra life.
        'R'_____________Sets time to 24.    'B'__Collect all BONUS letters.
        'T'_____________Sets time to 99.    'E'__Collect all EXTRA letters.
