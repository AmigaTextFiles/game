
        Hold down Left Mouse Button and Fire Button until game loads for
        unlimited lives.

        Press the 'CAPS LOCK' key and type "DECAFFEINATED" followed by
        'RETURN'.  The top of the screen should scroll, to let you know you
        have infinite lives.  Also, with the caps lock on type in "WEAPON0",
        "LEVEL0", and "LIVES0" to increase it.

        With the caps key on type any of the following during play.
            "I HATE THE RADION ADVENT"
            "SOMETHING SPOCK BUILT"
            "THE GNOME AT HOME"
            "WE LOVE THE PUNTENTAY"
