    
        On the level select screen, enter the door marked {SCREEN 2} The
        Horror Level.  After the level has loaded and before you press the
        fire button to start the level, push up on the joystick and hold
        down 'K'.  While still holding both, press the fire button to enter
        the level.  Once the level has started you can let go of the
        joystick and the 'K' key.  Now press 'ESC' to skip levels.
