
        On the option screen type one of these for the password:
            "REANIMATOR".................Gives access to levels 4-6.
            "PSYCHONAUT".................Gives access to levels 7-9.
        During play type any of the following:
            "I NEED OXYGENE"........................For full oxygen.
            "I NEED ENERGY".........................For full energy.
            "TRAINER MODE"..................For infinite everything.
