
        CHEAT #1--On the title screen, type "CHEAT MODE".  During play
                  press 'F2' to jump the next level.

        CHEAT #2--Pause the game with 'F9', then hold down 'SHIFT', '1',
                  and 'HELP'.  Unpause the game with 'F10'.  Again, use 'F2'
                  to skip levels.
