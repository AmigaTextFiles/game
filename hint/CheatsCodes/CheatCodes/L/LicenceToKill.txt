                              LICENCE TO KILL
                             =================


                                  LEVEL 1
                                 ---------

  If you can keep with the jeep and fire like mad, you should be able to
destroy it before it begins to twist through the narrow road between buildings.
Bullets fly all around so it's best to let Sanchez go (he got away in the film
after all). Use the time to pick off the gun turrets and then accelerate to the
end after the last bend (use the map).
