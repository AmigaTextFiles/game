
        When fighting against unbelievable odds dont panic.  Choose the long
        sword.  Keep using the slash-high attack to cause the most dammage.
        You may get down to only one man left,  but you can still defeat the
        enemy captain.  After you win, select send a prize crew so you will
        have enough people to sail your ship.

        Don't attack a nation your home country is allied with, it will
        upset both of them.

        Don't wed till you've got all your promotions, your oppurtunities
        will be much better.  Make conversation with all the governors
        daughters, however, they might gather important information for
        you from the governors mansion.
