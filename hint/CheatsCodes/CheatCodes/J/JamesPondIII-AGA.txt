
        On the map screen, type any of the following:
                "EVAS"____________________Save game at any time.
                "UNCLE ROGER"_______Message from the programmer.
                "FORMAT"_______Erase all previously saved games.
        During play, type "NIGHTMARE" and press 'F10' to access the cheat
        menu.  Lots of options to choose from.

        If you choose to play Finnius, you must pause the game and press
        'ESC' to restart the level.
