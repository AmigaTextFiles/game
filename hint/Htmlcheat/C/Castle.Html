NOTES AND HINTS

-THERE ARE 27 SPRITES INCLUDING THE DRAGON.THESE MUST BE KILLED BEFORE THE MAGISTER CAN BE KILLED
-MAXIMUM HEALTH IS 12 WEIGHTS (6 ON EACH SIDE) = HURCULEAN
-TO GET INTO CHAPEL ROOF STAND ON THE DRAWBRIDGE THEN CLOSE IT NOTE - DO NOT DO THIS UNTIL YOU HAVE THE KEY FROM THE WIZARDS HUT AND HAVE ENTERED THE CATACOMBS FROM UNDER THE LARGE ROCK,AND THEN UNDER THE RUG IN THE WIZARDS HUT
-LIFTING WEIGHTS IN THE GYM INCREASES YOUR HEALTH
-STANDING IN HOSPITAL CURES YOU UP TO NORMAL HEALTH
-THE SPIRIT IN THE COURTYARD WILL ONLY APPEAR WHEN A ROCK IS THROWN AT THE FLAG
-TO ENTER THE JUNK ROOM FIRE AT THE PLATE (ON THE LEFT OF THE DOOR) UNTIL IT DISAPPEARS,ENABLING THE DOOR TO BE OPENED
-TO KILL THE DRAGON,SHOOT EYES,TEETH AND HORNS UNTIL THEY DISAPPEAR
-TO KILL THE MAGISTER SHOOT AT HIS HEAD (NOT EYES)
-TO CONFRONT THE DRAGON,START ON LEVEL 4 IN THE SW TOWER.ENTER ONTO THE BALLROOM BALCONY,THERE IS A BREAK NEXT TO THE LAST DOOR,DROP DOWN,AND COMMENCE ALONG THE SOUTH PASSAGE
-TO FIND THE MAGISTER,GO TO THE SE TOWER AND PRESS THE BUTTON IN THE KINGS SOLAR ON LEVEL 4.THEN REACH ACROSS THE SECOND BARRIER IN THE CORRIDOOR AND ACTIVATE THE CRANK.GO ROUND THE SOUTH PASSAGE (THE LONG WAY) AND YOU WILL BE ABLE TO ENTER THE MAGISTERS ROOM
-A FINAL PIECE OF TREASURE APPEARS ON THE TABLE IN THE WIZARDS HUT AFTER THE KEY HAS BEEN COLLECTED FROM THE CHAPEL ROOF.USE ROCK TRAVEL TO TRANSPORT YOURSELF FROM THE WINDOW IN THE GYM TO THE WIZARDS HUT, THEN COLLECT THE TREASURE AND TRANSPORT BACK.
