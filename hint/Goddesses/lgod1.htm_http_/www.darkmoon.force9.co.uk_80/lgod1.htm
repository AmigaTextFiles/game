Go TO the GENTS and USE the TOILET, GET the STOOL then go OUT and WAIT. You are now captured by aliens and transported to Phobos and find yourself in a cell. EXAMINE the TRAY and GET the FOOD, PICTURE, BLANKET, STOOL and FLASHLIGHT. OPEN the DOOR and go out then SOUTH, OPEN the SOUTH DOOR and go in. GET the piece of PAPER and READ it, TELL TRENT "FOLLOW ME" and go to the Closet. LIGHT the FLASHLIGHT then DROP the STOOL, STAND ON the STOOL and GET the BASKET get DOWN then ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are now in the Jungle, READ the MATCHBOOK that Trent Gives You then go east to the Clearing. GET the CAN and go to the Back Door, OFFER the FLASHLIGHT TO the SALESMAN who gives you a machine then flies off on a giant moth. GET the MACHINE and KNOCK ON the DOOR. The door is answered by a mad professor and while he is locking the door go DOWN and THROW the CHOCOLATE INTO the CAGE. WAIT and the professor will strap you to a slab then transfer your mind into a gorilla in the cage. As a gorilla GET the CHOCOLATE AND the HOSE and then KISS the other GORILLA the professor will then leave. EAT the CHOCOLATE which will give you strength to BEND the BARS, get out of the cage and DROP the HOSE. UNFASTEN the STRAPS that hold your real body and PUSH the SWITCH. You are now back in your own body. GET OFF the SLAB (you automatically free Trent) and GET the HOSE. ENTER the CIRCLE and you are transported to the vidicom booth, PULL the KNOB then OPEN the BOX, GET the COIN you find there, go OUT and ENTER the CIRCLE outside.

You are at the Royal Dock on mars. Get onBOARD the BARGE and PRESS the ORANGE BUTTON. When a dock appears on the north shore PUSH the ORANGE BUTTON again and LEAVE the BARGE go NORTH and GET the BALM, EXAMINE the ALIEN then GET AND READ the MESSAGE then DROP the MESSAGE. Go back and BOARD the BARGE, PRESS the PURPLE AND ORANGE BUTTONS. When Docks appear on both sides of the canal and you are nearest to east bank PUSH the ORANGE BUTTON, LEAVE the BARGE and PUSH the ORANGE BUTTON again, the barge will now go off on its own. PUT EVERYTHING IN the BASKET and go to the Laundry, GET the clothes PIN and go the Sultans Room. ANSWER "YES" to the sultans Question then ANSWER "RIDDLE" to the Sultans Riddle. Go west and the guard will ask which wife you want, ANSWER The number from the message BACKWARDS and ENTER the HAREM, WAIT and the wife will appear, TELL the WIFE TO KISS YOUR KNEECAPS and you will be given a TORCH and a MAP.

The entrance to the catacombs will be revealed so go DOWN and follow the following route and actions to get successfully through them. NW, N, N, E, Clap, NE, NE, SE, Hop, Clap, Kweepa, D, NW, NE, Clap, N, S, Hop, Kweepa, Clap, NE, U, NW. GET the DIRECTORY, Clap, NW, Hop, S, Kweepa, Clap, SE, SE, D, E, Clap, Hop, N, W, Kweepa, Clap, E, W, S, Hop, Clap, SW. GET the RAFT, N, Kweepa, Clap, NE, Hop, E, Clap, NW, NE, SE, Kweepa, Clap, Hop, U, NW. You are now at the bottom of the well in the palace gardens so ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are at the barge at the Icy Dock. LEAVE the BARGE and DROP the MAP AND the TORCH, WEAR the BALM and go to the Penguin Park. GIVE the COIN TO the PENGUIN and get Change then go to the Tent and EMPTY the BASKET. GET and PUT the BABY IN the BASKET then GET the BLANKET AND COVER the BABY WITH IT. Go to the igloo and PUT the BABY ON the STOOP (You automatically hide), WAIT until the matron comes out and takes the baby inside. OPEN the DOOR and ENTER, GET the COTTON BALLS and go OUT then north to the tent and GET EVERYTHING you dropped earlier. Go to the South Pole and ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are transported to Watts up Dock. Go west, GET the MACHINE then STAIN the CIRCLE and DROP the CAN. Go to the Ruin and PUT clothes PIN ON NOSE, DROP EVERYTHING and PUT HANDS OVER EARS, CLOSE EYES and KISS the FROG. The frog will change to a Princess who will give you a Blender, leave all objects here and go to the Oasis. REMOVE the clothes PIN and DROP the PIN then ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are in Cleveland. Go south then GET the RAKE and the SACK and go to the Garden, MOVE the SOD to reveal a circle and GET the TRELLIS. Go to the Bedroom and GET the SHEET then TEAR the SHEET into STRIPS and TIE the STRIPS together. TIE the ROPE TO the BED and PUT the ROPE OUT OF the WINDOW. Let Trent climb down the rope and WAIT until he falls in from the ceiling. GET the HEADLIGHT then go to the Garden and ENTER the CIRCLE.

You in the Hallway on Phobos. Go to the Closet and ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are in the Jungle. Go to the Clearing to avoid the flytrap and when it gives up return west and COVER the HOLE WITH the TRELLIS, OPEN the SACK and COVER the TRELLIS WITH the LEAVES. Go west and when the flytrap comes after you go east and WAIT for it to fall in the hole. Go to the spawning grounds and GET the JAR, PUT EVERYTHING IN SACK and ENTER the CIRCLE.

You on the Spaceship. GET the SWORD and MOUNT the HORSE go west and DISMOUNT then GET AND WEAR the SUIT before OPENing the HATCH. Go through the hatch and repeatedly KILL THORBLAST WITH the SWORD until he drops his sword, GET THORBLAST'S SWORD and GIVE IT HIM BACK. KILL the MONSTER and FOLLOW the WOMAN to GET the PICTURE. Return to the horse and MOUNT the HORSE ride east and DISMOUNT. Return to the circle and ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are at My Kinda Dock. Go to the top of the tower and ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are in the Closet on Phobos, WAIT and the floor collapses and you are in the cell. Go to the roof and ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are in the desert on Mars. Go east, DROP the SWORD and PUT EVERYTHING IN THE SACK then GET ALL BUT THE SWORD and DROP the MATCHBOOK. Go to Hickory Dock and SHOW the PAINTING TO the MOUSE, the mouse freezes with fear so GET the MOUSE and go to the throne room. DROP the MACHINE then OPEN the MACHINE and PUT the JAR IN IT then SWITCH ON the MACHINE, OPEN MACHINE and GET the JAR. COVER the PRINCESS WITH the CREAM and she changes back into a human. King Mitre rewards you with a 82 degree angle, GET the ANGLE and go NORTH. PUT the RAFT IN the CANAL and BOARD the RAFT. WAIT until you see the dock on the south bank and GRAB the DOCK, go to the Exit Shop and BUY AN EXIT. GIVE the COIN TO the PROPRIETOR who drops a tube into the dust on the floor. RAKE the DUST to find the TUBE then DROP the RAKE AND the PAINTING. Go to Donald Dock and DROP the JAR then OPEN the TUBE and GET the CIRCLE, DROP both the TUBE AND the CIRCLE and ENTER the CIRCLE.

You are in the Leather Goddesses Boudoir. STAND UP and WAIT until the goddess pulls the lever and you fall down to the Plaza, Give the Objects to Trent as he asks for them, Trent then Builds the Anti-Leather Goddess Machine which will defeat the Goddesses and you are transported home.
