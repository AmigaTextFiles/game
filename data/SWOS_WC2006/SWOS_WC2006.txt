This is the update of teams to SWOS. Teams are adjusted to the World Cup 2006.
Please, bear in mind that this is just the first release. So far only
the names of the players, coaches and other small details has been modified.
Soon more to come.

If you find any inappropriate data, names with mistakes in spelling, wrong
positions of players, please let me know.

I would also like to receive any information about present skills and
value of any of the players of your country.

INSTALLATION
============

Copy files included into the archive into the "data" directory of your
SWOS game. Make sure you made a backup of your original teams.
Bear in mind that after the modification of the data files the game may not
work with WHDLoad.

History:
========

0.1 (24.06.2006)
- first release
  (only names of the players and coaches were adjusted to present day)
