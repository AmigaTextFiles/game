Please note that all files here (levels, landscapes and documents) are under heavy construction!

Well, since this is a very early version of the package, there's not too much to write about. Currently only featuring a half complete Wallace and Gromit landscape, I soon hope to have customs for each individual character, including Landscapes, Customs and even Grafitti levels. Enjoy!

I hope that some of you will contribute to this set. Without your help, these customs aren't gonna get much better..
